Underwood closes her fourth studio album , Blown Away , with this ballad in which she pours out her heart and soul to God .
It could also be interpreted as the narrator addressing her romantic obsession .
Mutt Lange penned the song .
Best known as the producer of rock heavyweights AC/DC and Def Leppard , Lange has also created plenty of more Country-orientated material for his former wife , Shania Twain .
