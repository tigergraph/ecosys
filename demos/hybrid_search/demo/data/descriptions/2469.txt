This song is about the euphoria of falling in love .
This was written in 1987 .
Ian Brown wrote the lyrics for the verses , while John Squire was responsible for the lyrics for the chorus .
This was The Stone Roses first UK Top 40 hit , reaching # 36 in July 1989 .
A reissue in March 1990 improved by 2 places .
Two music videos were produced .
One showed the band recording in a studio in January 1989 and the other one is taken from the Stone Roses ' gig at Blackpool Empress Ballroom on August 12 1989 .
