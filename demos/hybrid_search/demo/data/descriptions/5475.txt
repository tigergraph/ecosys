Primus wrote this song for the show South Park .
The band also wrote the theme song for the show , and this song was featured on the episode `` Chef Aid '' as well as the album that followed the episode .
`` Chef Aid '' was a bunch of musicians playing a concert for the release of the character Chef from prison .
On the show , before Primus performs , Les Claypool says : `` Chef inspired us to keep playing , no matter how much we sucked ! ''
The chorus of the song is sung by Isaac Hayes , who voiced the character Chef on South Park .
