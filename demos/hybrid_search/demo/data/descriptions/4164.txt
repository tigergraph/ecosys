For over 20 years , Conway Twitty had held the record for having the most # 1s on the Hot Country Songs chart , with 40 chart-toppers to his credit .
Then in September 2006 George Strait overtook Twitty 's mark when `` Give It Away '' advanced to # 1 , his 41st country song to achieve pole position .
This song was co-written by songwriting legend Bill Anderson and new Country singer Jamey Johnson .
