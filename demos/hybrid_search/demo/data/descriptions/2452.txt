This song that decries rumor and gossip is American R&B - soul singer -- songwriter Angie Stone 's lead single from her fifth studio album , Unexpected .
The track was co-written by vocalist and songwriter Juanita Wynn , who has been Stone 's collaborative partner for several years .
The song premiered on Stone 's website on October 5 , 2009 .
The song samples Evelyn `` Champagne '' King 's 1981 track `` What Are You Waiting For . ''
