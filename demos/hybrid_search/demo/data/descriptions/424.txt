During a concert in New York 's Carnegie Hall in June 2008 , Jill Scott shared a long on-stage kiss with her drummer , Lil ' John Roberts .
The pair then announced to the audience that they were engaged .
Jill soon fell pregnant and on April 20 , 2009 their baby boy , Jett Hamilton Roberts , was born .
Sadly , within a couple of months the couple had broken up .
This sparsely produced tune records their brief relationship and Scott 's heartbreak at it ending so `` quick . ''
