On this uptempo soul rocker Bailey Rae documents her recent traveling experiences and the rush of excitement when coming across somewhere new for the first time .
The singer was born and raised in the Northern English city of Leeds and until the age of 19 , she 'd never set foot on a plane , but now adores the excitement of discovering new places .
She told The Arts Desk : `` I love the architecture of Paris , the food , the culture , the hipness and irreverence of New York , and it was a kick flying from one to the other . ''
