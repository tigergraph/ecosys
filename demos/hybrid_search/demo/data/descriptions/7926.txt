This song was recorded by the band in two separate sessions , the first half , then the second half .
Said guitarist/keyboardist Anand Wilder to Complex magazine : `` It 's probably my favorite song on the album , actually .
I like this one a lot .
It 's just one of those songs .
I like the sound of it .
I feel like it 's a moment of uplift . ''
The song was first played live by Yeasayer during their May 21 , 2011 concert at Belly Up Tavern , Solana Beach , California .
