At first listen , this song could be a romantic love song to his wife .
In reality , it is a piece of advice that singer/songwriter Roger Clyne wishes the world would heed .
Clyne 's live shows and his music are infused with the message of peace and tolerance , and he makes a concentrated effort to spread the word without being preachy .
`` It seems like humankind has tried just about every other thing to try to get out of this obligation , '' he says , `` but I think that it might solve a lot of stuff . ''
