Delilah penned this song with the American songwriter and producer Reggie `` Syience '' Perry , who has won Grammys for his work on Mary J. Blige 's Growing Pains and Ne-Yo 's Because Of You .
The song was released as a single on September 17 , 2012 .
It includes a remix by UK garage innovator MJ Cole , who is according to Delilah , `` one of my all-time favourite producers . ''
Regarding the song 's meaning , Delilah said : `` Simply put -LRB- it -RRB- is about love .
Falling out of it !
The breakdown of a relationship , but learning to look forward as every cloud has a silver lining . ''
