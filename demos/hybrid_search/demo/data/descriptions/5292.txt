The title of this song is not a reference to slain South African anti-apartheid leader Stephen Biko .
Instead it is a term of endearment in Igbo , the Nigerian language that the parents of singer and lyricist Kele Okereke speak .
He explained to The Scotsman September 14 , 2008 : `` It means ` dear ' , like when you 're beseeching someone to do something . ''
