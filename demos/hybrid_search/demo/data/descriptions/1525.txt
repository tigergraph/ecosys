Pepper & Piano are a Manchester duo consisting of vocalist Katie Pepper and pianist Emma Alkazraji .
Good friends from college , Katie and Emma had never performed publicly together before their last minute decision to create Pepper and Piano for Sky 1 's Must Be The Music auditions .
The program is the UK 's first talent TV show to make it 's music available for download immediately and the duo 's heartfelt rendition of this original song resulted in the song quickly shooting up into the top ten of the iTune download charts .
