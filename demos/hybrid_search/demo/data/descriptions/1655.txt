This empowering ballad on Christina Aguilera 's fourth studio album , Bionic , was produced and co-written by her long-time collaborator Linda Perry .
The duo have worked on a number of successful songs together , including `` Hurt , '' `` Keeps Gettin ' Better '' and `` Beautiful . ''
Perry accompanied Aguilera , playing piano during the singer 's stripped-down performance of this song on the Hope for Haiti Now telethon that was held on January 22 , 2010 .
