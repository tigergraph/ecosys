Jennette McCurdy is an American film and television actress and singer-songwriter , who is best known for her role as Sam Puckett in the Nickelodeon show iCarly .
This is her first official radio single from her debut solo album .
Fans were given six song snippets from the record and then asked to vote on their favorite , which would be chosen as her debut release .
With almost 75,000 votes cast , this song was the winner .
Jennette told The Boot : `` This song really captures the last year of my life .
I 've been in Nashville writing for and recording my record and following my dream while really missing my family and friends back in Garden Grove , California . ''
