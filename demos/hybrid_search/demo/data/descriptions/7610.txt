This is the second single from Attention Deficit , American hip hop rapper Wale 's debut album .
On this track Wale tells of leaving his D.C. ghetto behind .
The song pays homage to A Tribe Called Quest 's `` Award Tour , '' from their 1993 album , Midnight Marauders .
The song features R&B singer Jazmine Sullivan .
However her contribution is limited to singing `` Ohh ohh '' at the end of each chorus .
