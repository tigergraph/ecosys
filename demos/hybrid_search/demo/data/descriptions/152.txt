This is the fourth single to be released from Flo Rida 's fourth album , Wild Ones .
Axwell , who is one third of the EDM act Swedish House Mafia , produced the track with the French duo soFLY & Nius .
The same trio were also responsible for Flo Rida 's `` Wild Ones '' single .
The video features comedian Nathan Barnatt , performing under the alter ego of Keith Apicary , being the life of the party and showing us how he rolls in Miami .
The song 's chorus incorporates a sample of blues guitarist Freddie King 's version of the classic tune `` Let The Good Times Roll . ''
