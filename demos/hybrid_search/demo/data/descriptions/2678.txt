This is the third single by rapper Mann , from his debut album Mann 's World , which was released via Los Angeles-based record label Beluga Heights Records .
Label boss J. R. Rotem produced the song .
The song contains a sample of American R&B - jazz-funk group Nu Shooz 's 1986 hit single , `` I Ca n't Wait . ''
An official remix featuring rapper 50 Cent was released digitally as a single on December 21 , 2010 .
It became Mann 's first song to chart on the US Billboard Hot 100 , entering at # 95 on the week ending January 29 , 2011 .
