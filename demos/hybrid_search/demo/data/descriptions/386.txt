This single by N-Dubz was recorded especially for the soundtrack to the dance film , StreetDance 3D .
The song was written and produced by the British hip hop group , along with dance DJs Bodyrox .
Some family relations in the band : Mungo Jerry bassist Byron Contostavlos is the father of Dino `` Dappy '' Contostavlos and uncle of Tula `` Tulisa '' Contostavlos , both members of N-Dubz .
This uses a sample of Johann Pachelbel 's `` Canon in D Major . ''
A number of other pop songs have borrowed from the Baroque classical piece .
They include , `` Hook , '' by Blues Traveller , `` All Together Now , '' by The Farm and `` Go West '' by both The Village People and The Pet Shop Boys .
