The Oregon-born , Nashville-based Mat Kearney recalled in publicity materials : `` During a recent visit home , I broke into the University of Oregon music room , which was under construction .
I jimmied the door open and climbed down this balcony .
I wrote ` Straight Away ' there . ''
Kearney added to Relevant magazine : `` Breaking into places to write on a piano is actually common for me .
I did n't own one until a year or two ago . ''
