`` She Falls Asleep '' is a 2-part song mainly performed by McFly 's lead singer , Tom Fletcher , about a young girl who commits suicide .
McFly 's 2008 album , Radio : Active , contains the sequel to this song , `` Down Goes Another One , '' told in the perspective of the girl 's love interest .
