Underwood penned the lyrics for this reggae-inflected song about finding your personal bliss .
She wrote the tune with frequent collaborators Josh Kear and Luke Laird .
According to Underwood , the whistling on this song was all her doing .
Speaking with Billboard magazine , Underwood shared that margaritas were served to loosen everyone up during the recording sessions for Blown Away .
We can hear the evidence on this breezy island-infused number as she sings , `` Raise your hands in the air tonight .
Put a little love back in your life . ''
