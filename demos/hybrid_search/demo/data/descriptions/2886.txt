This is the third single by American R&B singer Keyshia Cole from her fourth studio album , Calling All Hearts .
Cole performed the song on Conan on Jamuary 19 , 2011 and it was officially added to US radio on February 1 , 2011 .
R&B musician and producer Chink Santana wrote the song .
Amongst the other credits on his resume are co-producing Ashanti 's first two albums including the hit singles `` Happy , '' `` Rock wit U -LRB- Awww Baby -RRB- '' and `` Rain on Me . ''
He also co-starred in Keyshia Cole 's `` I Should Have Cheated '' music video as her cheating boyfriend .
