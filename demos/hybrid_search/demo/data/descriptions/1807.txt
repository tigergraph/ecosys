This romantic ballad is the third single from American R&B artist Lloyd 's fourth album King Of Hearts .
The song finds the singer crooning that he wants to be the one to kiss the girl he loves goodnight , before Trey Songz then Young Jeezy contribute verses .
The song was produced by Polow da Don , who was behind the recording console for the majority of the album , Lloyd told The Boombox that the producer challenged his voice .
`` To have somebody in the studio who is constantly pushing me and always demanding more of me vocally is great , '' he said .
