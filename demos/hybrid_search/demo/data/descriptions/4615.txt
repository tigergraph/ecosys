This is the third single by the American singer Dev from her debut album , The Night The Sun Came Up .
The track only features on the US edition of the LP and was released on December 20 , 2011 .
The sultry track features Spanish-Filipino pop music singer Enrique Iglesias .
Dev told USA Today : `` I like to think of ` Naked ' as the big sister of In The Dark , it 's more mature .
The song is a back and forth between a man and woman , and Enrique added flavor and Latin vibe to it . ''
The song was produced by the LA Valley Electro-Pop duo The Cataracs , who helmed the entire album .
