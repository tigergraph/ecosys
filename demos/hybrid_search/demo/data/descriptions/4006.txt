This ecstatic ode to love was written by Australian twins Miriam and Olivia Nervo with Finlay Dow-Smith .
The Antipodean siblings previously co-penned David Guetta and Kelly Rowland 's `` When Love Takes Over . ''
The song was Kylie 's fourth single from Aphrodite to go to # 1 on Billboard 's Dance/Club Play Songs .
Her previous three chart-toppers from the set were `` All The Lovers , '' `` Get Outta My Way '' and `` Better Than Today . ''
Kylie also topped the chart as a featured artist on Taio Cruz 's `` Higher '' during the same period .
