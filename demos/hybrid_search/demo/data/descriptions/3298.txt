This is the theme to the Nickelodeon TV series Drake & Josh , in which Drake Bell co-stars .
It was first released on the `` Drake & Josh '' soundtrack album with the title `` I Found A Way . ''
An acoustic version of this song appeared on Bell 's next album , It 's Only Time .
Special editions that came packaged with a bonus DVD included two videos about how to play this song on acoustic guitar .
