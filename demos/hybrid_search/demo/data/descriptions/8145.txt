This seasonal number was originally recorded by Connie Francis as `` I 'm Gon na Be Warm This Winter . ''
It reached # 18 in the US and # 48 in the UK in late 1962 .
This featured in television advertisements for the Co-operative retail chain in the UK over the 2008 Christmas period .
Cilmi 's version was included on the deluxe edition of her album Lessons To Be Learned .
