Shayne Ward was asked by Female First how `` Breathless '' reflected his personality .
The former winner of the UK talent show The X Factor replied : '' ` Breathless ' reflects my personality because people have seen the kind of emotional side of the ballad that I sang before , but they get to see a deeper side to it because the song is a deeper song , its one of the best songs I have heard in a long , long time , I 've said it to everyone , it is , it was written for me when you listen to the lyrics they are just so special .
It 's just a beautiful song so I think people get to see a deeper side to me . ''
