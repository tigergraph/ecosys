The track 's bluesy guitar loop was sampled from `` Where There 's A Will -LRB- There 's A Way -RRB- , '' a track from Los Angeles singer-songwriter D.J. Rogers ' 1973 eponymous debut album .
During a listening session for Undun at Manhattan 's Legacy Studios The Roots manager , Rich Nichols , explained the sample is the first and only the band have ever used .
In the past even the beats credited to Dilla , such as 2006 's `` Ca n't Stop This , '' were replayed by the band .
