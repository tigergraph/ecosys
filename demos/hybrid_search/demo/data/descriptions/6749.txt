This is an acoustic song with lyrics warning that love can lead to ruin .
The lyrics mention Samson , Marc Anthony , and King Edward , all of whom were brought down by the love of a woman .
Phil Beer played the fiddle .
He went on to form The Phil Beer Band and Show Of Hands .
Mick Jagger 's brother , Chris , helped with the historical references .
He is credited as `` literary editor . ''
