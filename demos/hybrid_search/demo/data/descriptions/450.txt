Romanian-born singer Inna , real name Elena Alexandra Apostoleanu , released this European electro-house track across Europe in 2009 and it quickly climbed the singles chart across the continent peaking at # 1 in Romania , Moldova , Bulgaria , Lebanon , Malta , Poland , Spain , Turkey , Russia , Hungary and Greece .
It also topped the Billboard Hot Dance Airplay chart .
The song was written and produced by the Romanian dance trio Play & Win and was recorded by Inna at their studios in Constanta , Romania in 2008 .
Lyrically , it describes a `` hot '' relationship when despite the narrator being separated from her lover her heart is still burning for him .
