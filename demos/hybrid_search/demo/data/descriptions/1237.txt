This mid tempo Rock song is a track from L.A.-based rock quartet Carolina Liar 's second album Wild Blessed Freedom .
The band described it to Artist Direct as a `` road song '' about leaving people that you care about behind when the band/tour lifestyle beckons .
They went on to explain that musicians are being forced to be `` honest , '' with the current popularity of Twitter and Facebook .
Nothing is private and `` wo n't be seen '' in this digital age of cell phone cameras and public tweets .
