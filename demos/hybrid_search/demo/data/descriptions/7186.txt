This was released as the first single from Pop-Punk band The Wonder Years ' third album ' Suburbia I 've Given You All and Now I 'm Nothing .
The song title was lifted from a newspaper headline in The Simpsons .
The song 's opening line is , `` The fountain was off , '' which is a reference to the band 's The Upsides track `` Logan Circle , '' in which Soupy sings , `` They turned on the fountain today at Logan Circle . ''
