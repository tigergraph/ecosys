This song by Californian rapper Snoop Dogg is a track from his 11th studio album , Doggumentary Music .
The song was produced by Scoop DeVille and features singer Marty James , who is the vocalist of the San Francisco-based alternative Hip-Hop group One Block Radius .
The song is a homage to Snoop Dogg 's home city of Los Angeles .
The rapper may have been inspired by his participation on Katy Perry 's tribute to Californian gurls .
