This song was originally composed for the movie Night at the Museum : Battle of the Smithsonian .
It was used in the film 's end credits .
The brothers explained to the Australian radio program The Hot Hits : `` After screening the movie and going through the initial process of matching our style to the visuals of the film , we wrote ` Fly With Me . '
It 's exciting to know that this song will be used in the film 's end credits , as well as featured on our new album . ''
