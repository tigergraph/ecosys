This is a track from British soul and R&B singer Beverley Knight 's sixth studio album , 100 % .
Knight explained on her website : `` It 's a ` go out and have a great time ' song !!! I wanted an anti-credit crunch , anti wallflower , feel good track , I think it hits the spot !
It 's the first disco-eque song I 've ever done . ''
DJ Munro and DC Joseph produced this song , which was also co-written by Munro along with Knight .
