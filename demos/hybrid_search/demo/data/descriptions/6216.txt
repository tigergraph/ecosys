This song was recorded during a February 1975 loft rehearsal .
It is about becoming disillusioned with hard drugs after they begin to take a toll on your life .
Peter Laughner , who co-wrote this song , lost his battle with drug and alcohol abuse in June 1977 when he died of complications brought on by his abuse .
This song has been covered by Guns 'N' Roses and Henry Rollins .
