We recommend this 27-and-a-half minute cut for those of you with a huge appetite for some Crazy Horse .
Many of the songs onPsychedelic Pill , including this one , came out of some extended jam sessions by Young with his backing band after the recording of their covers record Americana .
The song captures many of the frustrations and complaints Young addressed in his 2012 memoir Waging Heavy Peace , such as his disdain for the poor sound quality of MP3 players .
