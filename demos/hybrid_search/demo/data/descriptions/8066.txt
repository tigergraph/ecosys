A variety of themes run through Pop-Reggae artist Ziggy Marley 's fourth solo album , Wild and Free , including `` changes and revolution . ''
However the idea behind the disc 's first single lies closer to home , as it was inspired by the singer 's wife , Orly .
`` Social revolutions and group revolutions are good and we need that , '' Marley told Billboard magazine , `` but we also need personal revolution , revolution within ourselves that change who we are as people . ''
