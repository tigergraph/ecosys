This song is a look at ordinary people who became extraordinary , and is meant to inspire us regular folks - Jackson said , `` you can still be a hero even if you 're fat or skinny or have big ears . ''
Ray Charles gets a mention in this song as one of Jackson 's heroes , as he overcame his blindness to do amazing things .
Babe Ruth shows up because he was fat , but still became a great baseball player .
As for Jackson 's biggest hero , he says it was Beethoven .
