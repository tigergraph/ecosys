After coming fourth on the British talent show The X-Factor in 2008 at the age of 16 , Lancashire-born English singer-songwriter and actress Diana Vickers quietly signed to the RCA label .
She spent six months in a studio recording her debut album , Songs from the Tainted Cherry Tree , before playing the starring role in a West End production of The Rise and Fall of Little Voice .
After completing her theater stint , Vickers concentrated on launching her music career .
This song about not letting a boy hurt you is the lead single from Songs from the Tainted Cherry Tree .
It was penned by Cathy `` Ca n't Get You Out Of My Head '' Dennis and Eg `` Chasing Pavements '' White .
