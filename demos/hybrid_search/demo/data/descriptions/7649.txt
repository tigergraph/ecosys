The title of this song stands for `` Gangsta For Life . ''
This track , which sees Rihanna sprouting a series of bad-bitch boasts then purring at the end `` Revenge is sweet , '' was produced by London `` dubstep '' production duo Chase & Status .
Saul Milton , a.k.a. Chase , told BBC 's Newsbeat : `` This album for her is just about what she likes and what she 's into and what she wants . ''
