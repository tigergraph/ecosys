This is the first single from American alternative rock band The Hold Steady 's fifth studio album , Heaven is Whenever .
The `` J '' in the song title is a sweet waitress named Jessie , who has got herself mixed up with bad company .
The narrator decides she is too good for him and is concerned she is `` gon na crash into the harbor this summer . ''
Frontman Craig Finn told The Quietus regarding Heaven is Whenever 's lyrical content : `` The lyrics speak a lot about struggle and reward .
Its about embracing suffering and understanding its place in a joyful life . ''
