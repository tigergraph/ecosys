The four Little Mix girls wrote this song about the way their lives have changed after winning the eighth series of The X Factor .
Jade from the group explained it , `` was the first song we wrote and we 're really proud of it .
It represents our lives from X Factor to now and we hope it inspires our fans and makes them happy . ''
Little Mix wrote the song with Richard Stannard 's music production and songwriting outfit Biffco .
The team has worked with a number of high-profile artists , and was largely responsible for the rise of the Spice Girls .
