This warm and groovy country rocker finds vocalist Jeff Tweedy revealing a fine falsetto as he tells of his love for his beau .
`` I 'm simple as a matter of fact ... I still love you to death/And I wo n't ever forget how , '' he croons .
Tweedy 's teenage son Spencer directed the music video , which features appearances from fashion blogger Tavi Gevinson , Spencer 's little brother , Sammy , and his friend , Joey .
The song is one of the most pop-friendly tracks on Wilco 's eighth album , The Whole Love .
Tweedy described the tune to Spin magazine as , `` Other people 's pop music that does n't sound like pop music . ''
