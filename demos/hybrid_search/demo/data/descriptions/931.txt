This reflective Natalie Hemby , Kate York and Rosi Golan acoustic-based song sounds like a whispered prayer .
`` The first time I heard it I knew I wanted it on this record , '' said band member Jimi Westbrook .
`` It has one of the most beautiful and haunting melodies I 've ever heard - one of those songs that feels like it 's washing over you as you listen to it .
It 's one of my favorite things we 've ever cut . ''
