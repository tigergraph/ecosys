This was written in 1986 and intended for The Joshua Tree .
It was bumped by `` Trip Through Your Wires , '' which they felt was a better song to perform live .
U2 wrote this about Ireland , but they were also inspired by the American heartland .
It talks about the `` Mississippi delta sun , Mississippi and the cotton wool heat , Sixty-six a highway speaks . ''
Route 66 is a historic interstate highway in the USA .
