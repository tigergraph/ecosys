This is a track from American singer-songwriter Kris Kristofferson 's 20th studio album , Closer to the Bone .
Closer to the Bone marks some of the final recordings of Kristofferson 's friend Stephen Bruton , who passed away in May 2009 .
Bruton played guitar and co-wrote this track and the album is dedicated to the late guitarist .
Kristofferson told Billboard magazine : `` I ca n't think of Stephen without heartbreak .
We were soul brothers .
I 'm just grateful that he was able to play on this . ''
