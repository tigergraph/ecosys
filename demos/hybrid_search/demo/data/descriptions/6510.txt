The Maccabees are an English indie band .
This song is a track from their sophomore album Wall of Arms .
Frontman Orlando Weeks explained to The Guardian July 10,2009 : `` It 's about how my mum 's side of the family started .
My great-great-grandfather was a stablehand and he put all his money on a horse he 'd been training that was 17 hands high .
And it won .
After that , he could afford to marry my great-great-grandmother . ''
