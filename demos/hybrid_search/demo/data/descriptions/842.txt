Feeling raw after the break-up of a five-year relationship , New Jersey singer-songwriter Sharon Van Etten decided to move to New York to start all over .
This song describes her learning to love again .
Said the singer to Spinner : `` It 's OK to have doubts and fears , but you have to let go a little bit if you want to open yourself up again .
That song was about moving to New York to try to love again . ''
