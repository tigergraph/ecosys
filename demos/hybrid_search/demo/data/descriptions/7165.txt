This is the title track of Neil Diamond 's third Christmas album .
The song weaves into its lyrics a number of previous Diamond songs .
The ones we spotted are : `` Cherry Cherry '' `` Holly Holy '' `` Song Sung Blue '' `` Pretty Amazing Grace '' `` If You Know What I Mean '' `` I 'm A Believer '' `` Red Red Wine '' `` Sweet Caroline '' Diamond explained to The Daily Telegraph December 4 , 2009 : `` I thought it might be fun , a little novelty , just to squeeze in a bunch of my familiar titles as part of the story .
I thought it would be just fun and games , but it took me three or four months . ''
