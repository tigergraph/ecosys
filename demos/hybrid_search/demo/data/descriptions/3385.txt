This is based on `` Jane B , '' the B-side of Serge Gainsbourg and Jane Birkin 's 1969 hit single , `` Je T'Aime Moi Non Plus . ''
Spiteri explained to the Daily Record May 10 , 2008 that this was influenced by the breakup with her boyfriend Ashley Heath , the father of the singer 's daughter Misty Kyd : `` It 's about me finding salvation through this record .
Coming out of a long relationship , made me rethink my life and the one thing that kept me going was music . ''
