This is a track from New York rapper Fabolous ' fifth studio album , Loso 's Way .
The set is a concept album , inspired by the 1993 Al Pacino movie Carlito 's Way .
This song features R&B and hip hop artist Trey Songz .
On the track he sings , `` this is the last time but I got ta see my baby . ''
Fabolous explained to Billboard magazine that this , `` is a metaphor for him having to see the streets one last time , just like Carlito on his last run . ''
