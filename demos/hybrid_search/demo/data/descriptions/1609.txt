The son of a Greek artist father and Jewish South African academic mother , Foals frontman Yannis Philippakis told The Guardian May 7 , 2010 that some of the lyrics on this track refer to a period of family therapy , which involved three psychiatrists staring at the Philippakises from behind glass : `` And you 'd feel the pressure from behind the glass , being relayed into the room .
It felt like being in quicksand , '' he said .
The singer added that he found the process frustrating and pointless but did n't want to reveal more about what he called `` family mental illness . ''
