This was the second single to be released from the soundtrack to Plan B 's film Ill Manors , a semi-autobiographical tale concerning various characters struggling amidst crime and violence in Forest Gate in London .
The song was released July 2 , 2012 .
Plan B attacks in Ill Manors the prejudice of British society towards the underclass so its interesting to note that Shux himself comes from a privileged background .
He is a grandson of British diplomat Sir Evelyn Shuckburgh .
The song was produced by Plan B and Al Shux , who is best known for his Grammy-winning contribution to Jay-Z 's `` Empire State of Mind . ''
