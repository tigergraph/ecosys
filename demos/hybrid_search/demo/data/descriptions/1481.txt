This song is about Don Arden who was the former manager of Black Sabbath , and his daughter Sharon Osbourne , who married former Sabbath frontman Ozzy Osbourne .
This song features Tony Martin on vocals , Tony Iommi on guitar and Cozy Powell on drums .
At this point , Iommi was the only original member of the band .
