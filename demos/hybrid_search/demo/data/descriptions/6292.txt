Frontman Jason Wade penned this song for Allstate 's Teen Driving Program , which educates teens to drive safely .
It was made available through the US iTunes store on November 6 , 2007 , but was n't included on an album until Smokes and Mirrors in 2010 .
Wade dedicated the song to teens that have lost their lives in accidents and in particular to a friend of his that died in a car accident at the age of 16 .
