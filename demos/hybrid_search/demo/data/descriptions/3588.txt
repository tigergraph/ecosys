The song tells a moving story , which initially appears to be about the excitement of approaching fatherhood but ends with a twist when it becomes a tragic tale about a friend 's miscarriage .
Ed himself has said that this song is based on a friend of his losing her child in the fifth month of pregnancy .
According to some sources , her name is Katie .
The song 's music video finds Ed Sheeran sitting in a hospital waiting room and was shot all in one take .
Look out for a cameo appearance by actor Marcquelle Ward , who is known for playing BB in Britannia High as well as Mitch in several Coronation Street episodes .
He is the patient that gets helped onto the hospital bed at 1:49 .
