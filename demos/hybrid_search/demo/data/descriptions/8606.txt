K.R.I.T. told Complex magazine that this clap along street anthem , `` is about believing in yourself . ''
He added that it is , `` that high self-esteem where you feel like you got it all figured out or you feel super fresh or you feel your car 's super clean or you got the right kicks on . ''
`` And then utilizing all that energy and putting it into one record and making it super bass-heavy , but still incorporating a soul sample that moves , '' he continued .
`` I 'm not chopping up a sample and using the hook and going from that hook and using the guitar .
Nah , I 'm chopping up and making it into something totally different . ''
