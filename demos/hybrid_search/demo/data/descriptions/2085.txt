The American release of Tinie Tempah 's debut album , Disc-Overy replaced several tracks that were on the original UK release of the set .
This emotionally charged Stargate production is one of the new songs on the repacked US disc .
The song is a duet with Ester Dean and finds Tempah opening up about a failed relationship , while the fragile American songstress expresses her pain , singing , `` Does it make you happy / To see me down/Does it make you dead to see me drown . ''
