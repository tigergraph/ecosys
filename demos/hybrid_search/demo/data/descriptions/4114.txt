Singer Gordon Anderson told The Sun October 10 , 2008 that this 10 minute epic is `` lots of songs chomped together . ''
The song originally lasted half an hour before the band edited it down .
Anderson explained to The Sun : `` It was written for a girl called Bobby plus it reminds me of a Bobby Gillespie song in the Ivy Ivy Ivy-era of Primal Scream that I love . ''
