This was written by Ray Davies and first recorded by his band , The Kinks , in 1965 .
Van Halen 's first single was also a Kinks cover .
They released `` You Really Got Me '' in 1978 .
The tour to support this album was called the `` Hide Your Sheep Tour . ''
This is one of 5 cover songs on the album .
Van Halen released an album every year between 1978-1983 , and recording covers meant they did not have to write as many songs .
