This is the first single from System of a Down leader Serj Tankian 's third solo album , Harakiri .
`` It 's a comical political song talking about abusive capitalism and the differences created in economic stature and the reaction to it .
CEOs have gotten away with murder , until now , '' the Lebanese-born musician explained to Noisecreep .
The song was released on May 1 , 2012 .
