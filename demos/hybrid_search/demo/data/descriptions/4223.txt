Conor Maynard opens his debut album , Contrast , with this cut , in which he sings of a girl who is messing him `` up so good '' and tearing his heart apart , `` like an animal . ''
Conor wrote the song with The Invisible Men .
The London-based songwriting and production trio also collaborated with Conor on his first two singles , `` Ca n't Say No '' and `` Vegas Girl . ''
Maynard teamed up with rapper Wiley for a reworked version of the track , which was released as the fourth single from Contrast on January 21 , 2013 .
