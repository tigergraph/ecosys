Frontman Jimi Goodwin described this song to Billboard magazine as having a `` vaguely country rock-esque tune . ''
This song originated around the time of The Doves ' debut album in 2000 .
The band explained to the NME : `` It 's been hanging around since Lost Souls but we 've not worked on it before .
It just kept knocking on our door . ''
The Doves explained to the NME April 11 , 2009 : `` This song 's about a prominent place in Manchester . ''
