This song features Peter Gabriel on keyboards .
Gabriel also provides vocals on the chorus accompanying Robbie Robertson .
Richard Manuel , who was the Band 's pianist and sometimes vocalist , was found hanging in the bathroom of a motel room in Winter Park , Florida on March 4 , 1986 .
The Band had reformed and played in Winter Park the preceding night .
It later emerged that Manuel had suffered for many years from chronic alcoholism .
This song is an elegy to Richard Manuel , one of the original members of The Band who committed suicide in 1986 .
Eric Clapton 's `` Holy Mother '' on his 1986 album August is also a tribute to Richard Manuel as is `` Too Soon Gone , '' The Band 's tribute to Richard from their 1993 album Jericho .
The Robbie Robertson album won the Juno Award for Album of the Year in 1989 .
