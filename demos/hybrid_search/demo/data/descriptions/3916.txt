Dry The River are a folk rock band who are fronted by Norwegian Peter Liddle , who is a former medical student .
The other band members are guitarist Matt Taylor , bassist Scott Miller , violinist Will Harvey and drummer Jon Warren .
This is the opening track of their debut album , Shallow Bed , which was released March 5 , 2012 .
This is an old song of Liddle 's - it was previously titled `` Shallow Bed '' and was re-imagined by Dry the River for the album .
The singer explained to The Sun why he co-opted the song 's old name for their first record .
`` We liked the fact that the album would be titled under an old theme or idea , '' he said .
`` It seemed to pull together all of what we 've been through . ''
