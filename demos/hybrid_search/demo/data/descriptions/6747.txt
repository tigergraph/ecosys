This is the third single by American Country recording artist LeAnn Rimes from her thirteenth studio album Lady and Gentleman .
The single was officially released for digital download to iTunes stores on June 14 , 2011 and released to radio on July 18 , 2011 .
The video was filmed in Chicago in partnership with StandUp For Kids and ties in with the song 's message .
It 's intention is to raise awareness of the issue of homeless teens across America .
Rimes wrote the song with Connie Harrington , Sonya Isaacs and Jimmy Yeary . ''
` Give ' is a song with a message about how if we want love , kindness and happiness in our lives , then we need to give some of that back , '' she said .
`` I believe that message needs to be shared with the world . ''
