This is the first single by British indie rock band One Night Only from their self-titled second album .
The song 's music video features actress Emma Watson .
Lead singer George Craig told The News of the World that he got to know the Harry Potter star after they both featured in a Burberry modeling campaign .
He explained : `` We became really great mates and she asked to be in the video .
She wants to be a rock chick after ten years of Harry Potter . ''
