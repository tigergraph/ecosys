This is the title track and fourth single from American actor and singer Jamie Foxx 's fourth studio album .
The song was released on April 5 , 2011 .
American hip hop artist Wiz Khalifa comes in at about 1:50 on the song .
Foxx is not the first Oscar winner the Black and Yellow rapper has worked , with having previously collaborated with Three 6 Mafia .
`` Best Night '' is one of five tracks on Best Night Of My Life to be produced by Eric Hudson , whose previous credits include Ne-Yo 's single `` Can We Chill , '' and Kanye West 's hit `` Flashing Lights . ''
