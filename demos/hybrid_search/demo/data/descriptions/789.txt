Girls Aloud recorded this upbeat dance anthem as their comeback single after three years of hiatus .
Its release marked the tenth anniversary of Girls Aloud as a group .
The single was leaked online on October 15 , 2012 and got its first official play on Capital FM the following day at midday .
This was the official single for the 2012 Children In Need charity drive .
The song was crafted by Brian Higgins and his Xenomania production team , the writers behind all of the girls ' hit tunes .
