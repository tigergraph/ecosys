This song finds West boasting about his hedonistic pursuits .
It samples `` She 's My Baby '' by the Mojo Men , `` Stud-Spider '' by Tony Joe White , and `` Iron Man '' by Black Sabbath .
The song was co-written and co-produced by Mike Caren , who is an American Record Producer , and the Executive Vice President of A&R for Atlantic Records .
His other credits include contributing to the production of T.I. 's Grammy winning `` Swagga Like Us '' and co-writing Flo Rida 's hit single , `` Sugar . ''
