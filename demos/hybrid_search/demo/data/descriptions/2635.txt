Windel Edwards is a Jamaican reggae and dancehall singer .
He earned his `` Gyptian '' nickname from his habit of tying a shirt around his head and twisting his chin hair like an Egyptian pharaoh .
The official remix for the song features rapper Nicki Minaj .
Gyptian originally recorded this song in 2008 with production by Ricky Blaze .
It gradually gained heat Stateside and became the Jamaican 's first Hot 100 hit despite local island radio stations not supporting the tune .
Asked whether he was surprised at the reception that the song has been receiving in the US , Gyptian said , `` My song dem always a tek off outside of Jamaica before getting any attention in Jamaica .
So its not a surprise to me really . ''
