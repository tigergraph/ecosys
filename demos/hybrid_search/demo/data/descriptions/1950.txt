Of the eleven songs on the Unstoppable album , Rascal Flatts members co-wrote four .
Bassist Jay DeMarcus had a hand in this song about a girl who is n't quite ready to let go of her failed romance .
The other writers of this song were Michael Dulaney and Jason Sellers .
Sellers also co-penned `` Love Who You Love '' , `` Forever '' and `` She 'd Be California '' on Unstoppable .
