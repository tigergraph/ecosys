The first promo single from rapper Nelly 's seventh album was commissioned by sports network ESPN to accompany their `` Bowl Week '' college football coverage .
It finds the St. Louis native reflecting upon a successful athlete 's success , brought about by hard graft and teamwork .
The motivational tune also appeared on Nelly 's mixtape , O.E.MO , which was released on December 24 , 2011 .
Andrew `` Drew Money '' Thielk provided the beat , which uses marching band drums as a significant part .
Other credits listed on the producer 's resume include Jay-Z 's `` Hello Brooklyn 2.0 '' and Nicki Minaj 's `` Right Thru Me . ''
