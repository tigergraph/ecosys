This was released as a double-A sided solo single by Radiohead frontman Thom Yorke along with `` The Hollow Earth . ''
`` Feeling Pulled Apart By Horses '' was the original title of the Radiohead track Reckoner , a song that eventually emerged in a much different form as a track on In Rainbows .
Yorke explained through Radiohead 's online blog `` Dead Air Space '' that this song was co-written and played with Radiohead guitarist Jonny Greenwood .
He explained that it is `` a radical rework of an old tune that 's been kicking around without a home since 2001 . ''
Radiohead 's longtime collaborator Nigel Godrich produced this track .
