Samuel Adams Wisner , who recorded this under the name of Sam Adams -LRB- later Sammy Adams -RRB- , is a Boston , Massachusetts rapper .
Wisner is a direct descendant of founding father John Adams , hence his middle name .
Wisner released his debut EP Boston 's Boy of which this song is the lead single , on the independent 1st Round label .
He was still a senior at Trinity College in Hartford , Connecticut at the time and the recording surprised many by topping the iTunes hip-hop charts , beating out the likes of Lil Wayne and DJ Khaled .
The following week this song , which borrows from Annie Lennox 's hit `` Walking On Broken Glass , '' entered the Hot 100 .
