This song is about the first night Rod Stewart spent with his current wife Penny Lancaster at the Dorchester Hotel in London.English model and photographer Penny Lancaster first met Rod when he offered to let her take photos of him whilst on tour When the pair began dating it caused a large tabloid interest as Stewart had recently separated from his wife and mother of two of his children Rachel Hunter .
The pair married on June 16 , 2007 at La Cervara near Portofino , Italy and have had two children together , both boys .
