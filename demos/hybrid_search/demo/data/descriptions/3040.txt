This is the title track of American R&B singer-songwriter Anthony Hamilton 's seventh album , which was released on December 13 , 2011 .
Hamilton explained the title : `` We live our life , day to day , month to month , year to year , doing so much that we forget the love , the people around us , the things around us , and just life itself .
I want to remind people that it 's okay to be in love with yourself and the loved ones in your life . ''
