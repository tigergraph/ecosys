This somber meditation on the theme of the renewal of spring evokes an era of forgotten pastoral earthiness .
The song cycle of The Courage Of Others follows the changing seasonal moods .
Midlake 's singer and songwriter Tim Smith told The Independent February 5 , 2010 that he believes the cyclical nature of life , is a crucial element of art and life .
`` You see that in so many poems , '' he said .
`` William Blake , any of the older poets , it 's all very earth-centred and aware of the seasons . ''
This song and all but one of the ten other tracks on The Courage Of Others are in minor key .
This features a flute played by Tim Smith , who in his youth played the saxophone .
