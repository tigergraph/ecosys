Women + Country finds breakout alt-country singer Neko Case and her vocal partner Kelly Hogan contributing harmony vocals on eight of the album 's tracks .
The latter is employed as a full-time member of Case 's backing band and the pair recorded their vocals during an intense two-day session in Los Angeles .
On this pensive ballad Dylan and Case come close to a duet .
`` Neko 's a huge character , '' Dylan told Billboard magazine , `` she and Kelly add a huge personality to the record . ''
