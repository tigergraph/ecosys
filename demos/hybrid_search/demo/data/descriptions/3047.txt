This song , which features Kid Rock , is the second single from Pop-Rock singer-songwriter Uncle Kracker 's fourth album , Happy Hour .
Uncle Kracker commented to Real Detroit Weekly : `` Probably one of the most laid-back songs on the record .
` Good to be Me , ' in a nutshell , is my DNA . ''
This is one of two songs that Uncle Kracker wrote for Happy Hour with Brett James , the other one being `` I Hate California . ''
James , also co-wrote `` When the Sun Goes Down , '' Uncle Kracker 's 2004 hit with Kenny Chesney .
The version released as a single was a Country remake done for Uncle Kracker 's 6 song EP , Happy Hour : The South River Road Sessions .
The re-mix features and was produced by Kracker 's buddy , Kid Rock .
