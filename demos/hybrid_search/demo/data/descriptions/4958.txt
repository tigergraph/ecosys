This is one of four previously unreleased songs that was included on Good Charlotte 's Greatest Remixes album .
The others are `` Los Angeles World Wide '' , `` Fight Song '' , and `` War . ''
Singer Joel Madden said to Billboard magazine of this tune : `` We wanted to give our fans a new song .
It 's actually one of my favorite songs we 've ever written .
I like the vibe . ''
