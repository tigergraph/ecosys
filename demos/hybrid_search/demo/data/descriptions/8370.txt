Beth Orton wrote this song with Tom Rowlands , who is one half of the British electronic music duo , The Chemical Brothers .
The pair are longtime collaborators ; Orton first worked with Rowlands when she guested on the song `` Alive Alone , '' a track from The Chemical Brothers ' debut album Exit Planet Dust .
Orton explained to The Sun : `` Call Me The Breeze is a song about essentially becoming nature . ''
