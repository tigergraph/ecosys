Australian guitarist Orianthi Panagaris was chosen by Michael Jackson to be his lead guitarist for his This Is It concerts .
Sadly the King of Pop passed away before they began , but she played and sang at Jackson 's globally televised memorial , on July 7 , 2009 .
`` God Only Knows '' was a late entry to her debut major album release , Believe and Orianthi has said that this song about loss is about both the death of Michael Jackson and a friend Diane who passed away two months later at the age of 25 .
