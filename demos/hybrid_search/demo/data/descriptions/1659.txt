This classic retro ode to love features Cee-Lo of Gnarls Barkley , who also has a songwriting credit .
The other credited songwriters are Estelle , MC Jack Splash , John Legend and Drew Dixon .
Splash , who also produced the track is Matt Kahane from the group Plantlife .
Among his other credits are `` Teenage Love Affair '' by Alicia Keys and `` All Dressed in Love '' by Jennifer Hudson .
Splash also appears in the music video along with Aubrey O'Day from Danity Kane and the actors Jackie Long , Taraji P. Henson and Malik Yoba .
