This is the first official single by American rapper Birdman , from his fifth studio album Bigga than Life .
The single was released on November 22 , 2010 and features Lil Wayne with his first vocals since being released after an eight month stay in Rikers Island prison facility for weapons charges .
This song also appears on the duo 's collaboration album , Like Father , Like Son 2 , a follow up to their 2006 set Like Father , Like Son .
