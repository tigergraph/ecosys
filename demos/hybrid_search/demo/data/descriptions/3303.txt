Jack White wrote this on Marimba .
The concept of inserting random bits of noise actually started as an editing mishap - Jack insists on using the most primitive equipment and editing techniques .
While cutting the eight-track tape , Jack accidentally sliced the tape prematurely , and the rest , as they say , is history .
