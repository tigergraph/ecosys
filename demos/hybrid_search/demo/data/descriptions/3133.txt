This Eurodance song was a big hit for the Dutch group Vengaboys .
The driving electronic beat made the song stand out and gave the group their biggest US hit .
Crazy Frog remixed this for the album Crazy Frog Presents Crazy Hits , changing the line `` The Vengabus is coming '' to `` The Crazy Frog is coming . ''
