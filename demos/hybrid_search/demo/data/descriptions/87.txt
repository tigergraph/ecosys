This is the first single from Oklahoma native , Greyson Chance 's Truth Be Told Part 1 EP .
Greyson explained the meaning behind the song : `` Sunshine & City Lights is all about falling in love with your youth , '' he said , `` and staying young .
When I was writing the song , I was inspired by what I do with my friends back home and even being mischievous sometimes . ''
The Clarence Fuller directed video features Greyson with a girl as they take on the city .
The teen singer explained the clip is an extension of the song .
`` It 's about two Long Island kids sneaking out into the big city overnight , '' he said , `` having fun and being teenagers . ''
