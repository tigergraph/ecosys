This song was written about the actor/writer/playwright Sam Shepard during Bob Dylan 's Rolling Thunder Revue tour .
Sam Shepard wrote The Rolling Thunder Logbook , which is an account of the tour .
The `` woman at home '' in this song is Patti Smith , who declined the invitation to join the musicians on the Rolling Thunder Revue .
