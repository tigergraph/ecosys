Smith explained during an album playback that this is , `` a jam off a Sun-Ra phrase , ` The Second Stop Is Jupiter . ' ''
She added : It 's named after Andrei Tarkovsky 's film , Ivan 's Childhood , the most beautiful movie about war .
Both Smith 's children from her marriage to Fred `` Sonic '' Smith - Jackson and Jesse Paris - play on this track .
Jackson , who would go on to marry The White Stripes drummer Meg White , contributes guitar and Jess the piano .
