This bass-heavy track takes its name from the street slang for a drug prescribed for ADHD .
It was all the rage among arty Manhattanites around the time Bloc Party were in New York recording Four .
`` I 'd be at parties and people kept telling me about it because it keeps you super-focused , '' frontman Kele Okereke told Q magazine .
`` But I noticed the energy coming off them was neurotic and aggressive , which is what Team A 's about .
We wanted to call the song by its real name but we 'd probably get sued .
`` The drug in question is Adderall , which is a brand name owned by Shire Pharmaceuticals .
