I Killed The Prom Queen are an Australian band from Adelaide formed in 2000 originally called Child Left Burning .
They were one of the first Australian bands on the Hardcore/Metalcore scene .
The band spilt on April 7 , 2007 after the departure of many vocalists over the years .
