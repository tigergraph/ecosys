Kodaline were formed in the quartet 's small Irish hometown of Swords and performed regularly in nearby Dublin .
Frontman Steve Garrigan told The Sun that this song , `` is about the band leaving the town and seeing the world . ''
The song was released as part of an EP , which also included an acoustic version of the track featuring Scottish songwriter Nina Nesbitt as well as a cover of Roy Davis Jr 's 1997 house hit , `` Gabriel . ''
Speaking about the collaboration with Nesbitt , guitarist Mark Prendergast said `` We first met Nina in LA , when she supported us at the Hotel Cafe .
After that , we kept running into each other at shows and became friends .
When we started planning the Brand New Day EP , the idea of collaborating on a track came up and Nina was the natural choice . ''
