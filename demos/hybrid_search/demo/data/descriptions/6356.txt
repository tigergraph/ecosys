This is the first single from American singer-songwriter and American Idol runner-up Blake Lewis ' second album , Hearbreak On Vinyl .
Lewis explained on his official blog that that album title , `` is an ode to all the record stores that have been closing down due to my industry eating itself .
These are the stores that brought inspiration to all of us music lovers and have led me to make the music I create today . ''
The 1940s film-noir style music video , which tells the story of the end of a couple 's relationship , stars Lewis and Casey Carlson , who was also a Season 8 American Idol contestant .
