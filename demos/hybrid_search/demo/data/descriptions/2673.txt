This song was written by British singer-songwriters Paul Carrack and Nick Lowe and originally featured on Lowe 's 1994 album The Impossible Bird .
Carrack subsequently covered it as well as Nanci Griffith and Diana Ross .
Hicks explained to the Birmingham News : `` I see this as the traditional blues song on the record .
It 's got great lyrics and a great story .
I thought that I would love to be able to tell that story in my own way . ''
