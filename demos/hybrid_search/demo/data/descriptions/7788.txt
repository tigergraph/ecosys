This is the first single from Iranian-American house DJ and producer Sharam 's solo album Get Wild .
Sharam is also one half of the house music duo Deep Dish , who had a # 3 hit in the UK in 2004 with `` Flashdance . ''
The song features American rapper Kid Cudi on vocals .
Cudi said : `` I was like wow , this is really , really innovative .
I have n't heard anything like it ... the Patsy Cline sample and how he blended it together and made it hip-hop too . ''
On this song Sharam mashes genres by sampling C&W star Patsy Cline 's `` Strange . ''
