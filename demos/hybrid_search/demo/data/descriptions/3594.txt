Nelly described in publicity materials her fifth studio album The Spirit Indestructible as , `` raw and honest - a friendly punch in the face . ''
Asked by BBC Breakfast why she compared the album to a `` friendly punch in the face , '' she explained : `` It 's a friendly punch in the face because a lot of the messages are super positive .
There 's songs about love and hope and inspiration .
There 's a song called ` Miracles ' about just getting swept away in positivity . ''
