This was featured in the movie Over The Top , starring Sylvester Stallone as a championship arm wrestler .
In the music video , Hagar and Stallone arm wrestle .
Hagar wins .
Hagar and Eddie Van Halen wrote this with the help of songwriters Giorgio Moroder and Tom Whitlock .
Moroder and Whitlock wrote many movie hits of the '80s , including songs from the movies Flashdance and Top Gun .
