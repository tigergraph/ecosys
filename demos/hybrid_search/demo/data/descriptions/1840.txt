On this Texas-dancehall jump , the character sends messages to three sisters in Dallas ; two receive friendly greetings but the third is warned to `` Pray the Sinner 's Prayer . ''
In an interview on his website , Dylan was asked what he meant by the Sinner 's Prayer .
He replied : `` That 's the one that begins with `` Father forgive me for I have sinned . ''
Los Lobos ' David Hildago , whose accordion was a signature sound on Together Through Life , told Uncut magazine January 2010 about the recording of this song : `` It started out like a Jimmy Reed tune and it ended up ... Bob was playing organ , he started this riff , and it went from this completely other thing , to what it is now .
It was fun to be in the room when it happened . ''
