The songs on The Circle were inspired by the events in the second half of 2008 , when the banking crisis threatened a worldwide economic catastrophe and America elected a black president .
Jon Bon Jovi told The Guardian November 5 , 2009 : `` The themes were based on what was happening in the world .
When you read into the lyrics of Can We Be Happy Now , that 's our president getting elected . ''
Jon Bon Jovi told Q magazine December 2009 that he wrote this confessional rocker for Barak Obama .
He added : `` we wrote it the day after he got elected .
I want to take it in and play it to him . ''
