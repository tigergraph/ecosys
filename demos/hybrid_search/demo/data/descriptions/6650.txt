This is the only song in the Camp Rock movie credited solely to the Jonas Brothers .
Kara Dioguardi and Mitch Allan wrote this .
Among Dioguardi 's other songwriting credits are `` Pieces Of Me '' by Ashlee Simpson , `` Taking Chances '' by Celine Dion , `` Ai n't No Other Man '' by Christina Aguilera and `` Spinning Around '' by Kylie Minogue .
Mitch Allan 's previous biggest success had been co-writing Bowling for Soup 's 2004 hit `` 1985 . ''
