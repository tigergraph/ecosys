This inspirational song is the first single from actress and R&B / soul singer Fantasia 's third studio album .
The 2004 American Idol champ first introduced it on her VH1 docu-series Fantasia For Real .
She later performed it on The Oprah Winfrey Show on February 3 , 2010 .
Written by Heather Bright and produced by The Stereotypes , the song illustrates the steps Fantasia made to get her life back on track after various events that led to the mismanagement of her career .
They include missing numerous Broadway performances of The Colour Purple due to a tumor on her vocal cords .
