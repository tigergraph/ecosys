A fair proportion of Grace/Wastelands was written in Paris , including this song , which credits Doherty 's former Libertines bandmate , Carl Barat , as co-writer .
Doherty told Clash Magazine that Barat contributed the line , `` Your boyfriend 's name was Dave / I was bold and brave . ''
In addition , Doherty told the NME magazine that `` the title is the title of a novel Carl 's sister wrote . ''
Then added , `` there 's misogyny , hotel rooms and weird positions . ''
