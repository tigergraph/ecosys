This mainstream neo-traditional love song was written by well-established Nashville songwriters Jim Collins -LRB- Keith Chesney 's `` The Good Stuff '' and Paul Overstreet -LRB- Keith Whitley 's `` When You Say Nothing At All '' -RRB- .
The song was previously recorded by Alan Jackson on the 2008 Billy : The Early Years movie soundtrack .
Country singer-songwriter Vince Gill sings harmonies on the chorus .
