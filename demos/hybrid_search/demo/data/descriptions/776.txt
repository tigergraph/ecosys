Originally released as a slow ballad , this was re-recorded as a fast-paced duet with Christina Aguilera in 2001 .
Separate copies the duet were included with versions of Sound Loaded that were already pressed with Martin 's solo version .
After the duet with Aguilera was recorded , it replaced Martin 's solo version on Sound Loaded .
People who had already bought the album were allowed to download the new version or send away for a free copy .
Martin performed this on January 8 , 2001 on The American Music Awards .
