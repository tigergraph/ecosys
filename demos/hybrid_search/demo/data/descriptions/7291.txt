This is based on a haiku by the Japanese poet Basho -LRB- 1644-1694 -RRB- .
A haiku is a 17-syllable poetic form with lines of 5 , 7 and 5 syllables .
Basho is recognized as the master of the haiku and it was he who made it the accepted form of poetic expression in Japan .
The Japanese poet wrote Sumiregusa while he was travelling to Otsu .
On his way through the mountain road the sight of a wild violet touched his heart .
