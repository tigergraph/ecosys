This is the lead single from McFly 's second greatest hits collection Memory Lane : The Best of McFly .
The track is one of three original songs on the collection .
Vocalist Tom Fletcher told Digital Spy : `` We recorded the song a while ago and were keen to put it out as a single .
It was one of the few songs where there was no disagreement between us or the label that it should be released . ''
The song debuted at # 10 on the UK singles chart , becoming the band 's 18th top 10 hit out of their 20 entries .
Fletcher told The Daily Star that he wrote the song on a ukulele .
