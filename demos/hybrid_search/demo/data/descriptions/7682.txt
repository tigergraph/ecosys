In this melancholic and nostalgic number former Stone Roses vocalist Ian Brown looks back at his days with the Manchester band and in particular his relationship with guitarist John Squire .
Brown co-wrote this with a Japanese musician called Naoto Hiroyama , who 's in a band called Orange Range .
He explained to Clash Magazine that Hiroyama gave him the music , `` we did our own drums but he gave me the keys and the guitars . ''
