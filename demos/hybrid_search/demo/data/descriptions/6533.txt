This is the fifth single by English rapper Tinchy Stryder , from his second album , Third Strike .
The song features guest vocals from Canadian singer Melanie Fiona .
Some of you may not be au fait with the R&B / soul songstress .
Check out the haunting `` It Kills Me , '' which peaked at # 43 in the US or `` Give It To Me Right , '' which reached # 41 in the UK .
The song was produced by Bradford born/London based knob twiddler Nathan Retro , who has also worked with Chipmunk , McLean , Tinie Tempah , Wiley and JLS , not to mention international superstars Shontelle and Flo Rida .
