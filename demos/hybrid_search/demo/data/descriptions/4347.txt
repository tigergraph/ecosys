`` Hold It Do n't Drop It '' samples the bassline from Tavares ' 1975 hit `` It Only Takes A Minute . ''
`` Hold It Do n't Drop It '' topped both the Brazilian and American dance club play charts .
This was produced by Midi Mafia , who are Canadian Producer/Engineer/DJ Dirty Swift , and American Producer/Songwriter/Artist Bruce Waynne .
Other hit singles they 've been involved with include 50 Cent 's `` 21 Questions '' and Fantasia 's `` When I See You . ''
