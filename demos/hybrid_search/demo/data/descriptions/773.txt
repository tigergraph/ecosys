This song was released on November 12 , 2012 as the first single from the Welsh Post-Hardcore band Funeral for a Friend 's sixth album , Conduit .
Bassist Richard Boucher told Alternative Press magazine why this was chosen to lead the set : `` This is the track that probably best represents the sound of the album , '' he said .
`` It has all the other aspects of the other tracks in one .
Fast-paced , aggressive , and catchy with some tasty guitar riffs . ''
