The Wildhearts are a rock group originally from the north east of England .
This is a track from their eighth studio album , Chutzpah ! .
This song is a tribute to Tim Smith , the leader of the highly influential English Alternative Rock band , Cardiacs .
Wildhearts frontman Ginger explained to The Sun August 28 , 2009 : `` Tim is one of the last true geniuses alive , and the fact a media obsessed world has little interest is n't a shock . ''
