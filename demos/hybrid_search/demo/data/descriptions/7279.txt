A Public Affair was Jessica Simpson 's first album after her high-profile divorce from Nick Lachey .
The song describes a night out among famous women who enjoy the attention their presence brings .
Lots of famous people appear in the video : Christina Applegate , Christina Milian , Eva Longoria Parker , Maria Menounos , Andy Dick and Ryan Seacrest .
The background arrangement was lifted from the Diana Ross song `` Ai n't No Mountain High Enough . ''
