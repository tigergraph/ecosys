On this track West sings about the journey his life so far has taken him .
He knows his destination , but he 's `` just not there . ''
Canadian singer-songwriter Esthero provides the female backing vocals and has a songwriting credit for this song .
Esthero also co-penned `` Robocop '' and `` Love Lockdown '' on 808s & Heartbreak .
She 'd previously provided the voice of the spaceship J.A.N.E. on West 's Glow in the Dark tour .
