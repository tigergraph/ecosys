This was written by Motown hit-maker Smokey Robinson and marks his first collaboration with Mary Wells .
Wells plays the part as the `` one who really loves you '' and tries to convince her boyfriend to ignore the siren songs of other lovers .
The backup vocals were provided by a male group called the Love Tones , while the instruments were played by the Motown house band Funk Brothers .
