TFK frontman/songwriter Trevor McNevan admitted to NewReleaseTuesday that this song , `` may catch some people off guard at a first glance , '' but he added that is what he loves about it .
He explained : `` I 've never been a fan of one-dimensional records , and each time we enjoy pushing the boundary further as to who TFK is .
You have to follow your inspiration .
I love this song , and am excited to share it with you .
It 's an honest love song that hopefully people will be able to feel and step into .
Love is vulnerable , and I think this paints that picture well .
To me , this is a worship song . ''
