The tracks on The Circle were inspired by various news events in the second half of 2008 .
On October 24 , 2008 , R&B / soul singer Jennifer Hudson 's mother , Darnell Donnerson , and brother , Jason , were found shot to death inside Donnerson 's Chicago , Illinois home .
Jon Bon Jovi told The Guardian November 5 , 2009 that this song , `` was about Jennifer Hudson 's family getting shot on that Sunday morning .
The stories you read in the lyrics are all true . ''
