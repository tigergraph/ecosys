This sassy put down to an over confident ex is the second single from British girl group The Saturdays ' second studio album .
The song was co-written by Norwegian songwriter Ina Wroldsen , who co-penned seven tracks on Wordshaker .
The other co-writer was English producer Steve Mac , who has credits on numerous UK # 1 singles and albums for artists such as Westlife , Gareth Gates and Leona Lewis .
