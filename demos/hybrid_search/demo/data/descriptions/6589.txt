This upbeat dance track features Sean Paul singing of a failing relationship .
`` Baby you do n't have to keep looking through my phone If something 's going on , girl , you 'd already know .
We ca n't even say two words without a fight anymore .
How did we get here ?

The song was released as the first single off Sean Paul 's sixth album on June 10 , 2013 .
This Electro ditty was written by Sean Paul , Benny Blanco , The Cataracs , and was produced by the latter two .
