Young Money is a rap supergroup of sorts comprising Lil Wayne , Drake , Gudda Gudda , Mack Maine and Jae Millz .
Though not signed to Young Money when he recorded this , Canadian rapper Drake is one of the featured artists on this track .
Until this song leapt 34-10 in the Billboard Hot 100 , Drake had not notched a top 10 hit .
In the same week as this song arrived in the chart , his solo single , `` Best I Ever Had '' vaulted 27-3 , resulting in him achieving the rare feat of debuting in the Top 10 both as a solo artist and as a group member .
Young Money Entertainment is an American record label founded by Lil Wayne and this was the first single from a compilation album featuring Weezy 's Young Money crew .
