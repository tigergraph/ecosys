The Band Perry were the first act to have a Gigi 's Cupcake named after them .
The ` Very Perry Chocolate Cherry ' cupcake was inspired by a lyric from this song .
`` When we heard that we might have a Gigi 's cupcake named after us we knew right away what the flavor would be , '' said Kimberly Perry .
`` There is a song on our new album that has a line in it that says , ` I 'm gon na change my name to Cherry ... , ' so we decided that the cupcake had to be some kind of a cherry cupcake . ''
