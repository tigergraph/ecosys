This musical suicide note was released as the third single from American Capitalist .
The song was Five Finger Death Punch 's thirteenth single overall .
Ivan Moody sings on the chorus , `` Step away from the ledge .
I 'm coming down . ''
The band partnered with the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline in conjunction with the single 's release .
Part of their promotion , which is aimed at awareness about suicide prevention among FFDP 's fans , involves the song 's music video , which concludes with a call to action alongside the lifeline 's national phone number .
