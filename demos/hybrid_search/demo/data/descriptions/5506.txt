This is a track from Christian rock band Hawk Nelson 's fourth studio album , Live Life Loud .
In 2008 , Hawk Nelson went to Haiti to visit the Hands & Feet Orphanage , where the quartet was moved by the people they met , especially children .
This song was written following this visit , and the band have said in interview that the song was given even greater meaning after the country 's devastating 7.0-magnitude earthquake in January 2010 .
