Kelley wrote this song with Craig Wiseman and his producer Clint Lagerberg .
He met Katherine Keigl when she played the love interest in his 2005 video for `` Only You , '' and they were married in 2007 .
Kelley told us the `` Learning You '' is one of his favorites from the Georgia Clay album , and said : `` It 's kind of a reminder to make sure that you keep passion alive .
We 've been together for six years now and it 's like , I want to make it so that there 's always something new to learn , so that keeps the spark alive . ''
