Del Rey declares her undying love for her troubled beau on this song .
The singer penned the melancholic tune with San Francisco songwriter Rick Nowels , the co-author of Belinda Carlisle 's `` Heaven Is A Place On Earth '' and Colbie Caillat 's 's `` Fallin ' For You . ''
The song was released as the sixth single off Born To Die in Germany , Austria and Switzerland only .
The song 's blippy beats were supplied by hip hop producer Emile Haynie , best known for tracks by Kanye West , Kid Cudi and Eminem .
