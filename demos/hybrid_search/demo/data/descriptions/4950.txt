This track was originally titled `` Bomb the Pentagon , '' and debuted as part of Primal Scream 's live set in summer 2001 .
In light of the September 11 , 2001 attacks , both the lyrics and title of the song were reworked , and the revised version appears on Evil Heat .
Frontman Bobby Gillespie explained to Q magazine : `` It was about the kind of pornographic images of war and how it 's sold to people .
So it 's not an anti-American song .
It 's an anti-military industrial , anti-war song . ''
