This is a track from Welsh rock band Stereophonics ' seventh studio album , Keep Calm and Carry On .
This song refers to an incident in singer Kelly Jones ' childhood , when the street in his Welsh hometown of Cwmaman flooded .
He recalled to Q magazine November 2009 : `` I remember our attitude at the time was , Well , we 've just got to get on with it .
That feeling of positivity runs through the whole record . ''
