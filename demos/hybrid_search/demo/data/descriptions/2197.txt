Otto Jettmann is a Swedish DJ and music producer who records under the name of Otto Knows .
His first break came in 2010 when his remix of Imogen Heap 's `` Hide And Seek '' caught the attention of EDM artist Avicii , who used his production in `` Hide & Seek -LRB- Avicii Remix -RRB- . ''
Jetmmann achieved his first Europe-wide hit with this song .
