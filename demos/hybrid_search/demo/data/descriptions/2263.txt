This club banger was co-written by Taio Cruz who was asked to pen two numbers for Cole .
The other track he wrote was `` Break Your Heart , '' but there was a delay in her management deciding whether they wanted the song or not .
Cruz reclaimed it , and released the track as the lead single from his second album Rokstarr .
`` Break Your Heart '' went on to be # 1 hit in the UK .
