Eddie Rabbitt was a Country singer and songwriter , but this was a huge crossover hit , going to # 1 on the Pop , Adult Contemporary and Country charts .
The song is about the peaceful and cleansing feeling a thunderstorm can bring .
-LRB- thanks , Bertrand - Paris , France -RRB- According to The Billboard Book of # 1 Hits , Rabbitt first got the idea for the song in the '60s when he was sitting in his small apartment on a rainy night .
He sang , `` I love a rainy night , I love a rainy night '' into a tape recorder , but did n't complete the song until 1980 , when he discovered the tape in his basement .
He finished the song with the help of fellow songwriters Even Stevens and David Malloy .
