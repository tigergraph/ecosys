Don Thomas released this classic Northern soul track in 1973 .
Thomas , whose real name is Charlie Thomas , was a member of The Drifters between 1969 and 1971 .
A remixed version by Ian Parton was used in a 2009 Visa TV advert .
The commercial features dance and media artist Bill Shannon , who was born with a degenerative hip condition .
Shannon has developed a unique dance technique on crutches , which he utilizes in the advert .
