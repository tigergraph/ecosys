Norwegian production duo StarGate produced this song , which samples `` Intro '' by The xx .
`` Intro '' is the opening track of the British Indie Rockers ' self-titled debut album , which spent eighty-seven weeks on the UK Albums Chart and won the 2010 Mercury Prize , an annual music award given to the best long player from the United Kingdom .
Longtime collaborator Ester Dean penned this song , which utilizes the oft-used love-as-addiction metaphor .
The most obvious example of another song with the same topic is Robert Palmer 's `` Addicted To Love . ''
