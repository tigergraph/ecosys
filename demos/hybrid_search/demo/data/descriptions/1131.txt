In January of 1996 Buffett 's airplace nicknamed the `` Hemisphere Dancer '' was shot at by Jamaican police who thought that the plane was smuggling marijuana .
The plane suffered slight damage .
With Buffett on the plane at the time of the incident were U2 frontman Bono and Island Records producer Chris Blackwell .
The Jamaican government apologized for the mistake .
