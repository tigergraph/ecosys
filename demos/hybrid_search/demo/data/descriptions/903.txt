This song was originally recorded by the Australian singer Missy Higgins .
Released as the second single from her 2007 On a Clear Night album , it peaked at # 10 on her home country 's ARIA chart .
Rimes covered this for her 2013 Spitfire album .
She noted during a live preview of the record that a friend first played the tune for her , while she was going through her divorce from Dean Sheremet .
`` I could n't have written it any better , '' she said .
