This is the opening track from the Swedish indie rock band Peter Bjorn and John 's sixth album Gim me Some .
Drummer John Eriksson told Spin magazine regarding the sessions : `` We were more drunk this time .
We have to blame the producer for that .
He made us drink more .
A lot of the final cuts were the drunken versions .
`` Eriksson added that this song is `` about getting drunk because that 's what we were during recording . ''
