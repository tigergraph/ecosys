This is one of two new songs recorded by Contemporary Christian singer-songwriter Plumb for her greatest hits collection , Beautiful History : A Hits Collection .
A second disc was included featuring eight dance remixes , including one for this song which climbed to the top of Billboard 's Dance Club/Play Songs chart .
Plumb told Billboard magazine that her chart-topping success is rewarding on both a personal and professional level .
She said : '' ` Hang On ' was written about finding hope when you feel hopeless .
For it to be # 1 is not just a fantastic feeling , it 's incredibly satisfying knowing I gave my fans what they wanted . ''
