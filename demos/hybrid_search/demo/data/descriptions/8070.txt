Sage The Gemini is a rapper from Bay Area , California , who first gained notoriety when both this cut and `` Gas Pedal '' went viral on the internet .
The song title refers to the strain of Pit Bull Terriers known as Old Family Red Nose .
Pit Bulls are notorious for biting , and like other dogs , they shake things as they chew .
Sage is asking here the girl to shake her rear like a Red Nose Pit Bull shakes its head .
