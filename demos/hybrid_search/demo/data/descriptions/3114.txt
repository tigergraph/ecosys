Written by band members Alex Davies and Ed Minton after watching a bunch of her movies , this song is an ode to the actress Jessica Alba .
Mentioned in the lyrics are Alba 's movie Sin City and her TV series Dark Angel .
Why did they decide to write a song about Abla ?
The band explained in a press release : `` She 's hot . ''
`` Jessica '' was the second single for Elliot Minor , following up `` Parallel Worlds , '' which hit # 31 in the UK .
The band members are classically trained and were all members of choirs in high school .
This track demonstrates their classical background , with strings , piano and orchestral accompaniments .
