This is a track from Field of Reeds , the third album released by the British band These New Puritans .
Speaking about the recording of the record , Jack Barnett of the group said : `` I like the sense with this music that you are surrounded by people playing instruments .
You can hear someone move or breathe before they begin playing . ''
He added : `` For the song ` Fragment Two , ' George 's drumming is take # 76 . ''
