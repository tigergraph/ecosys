This is a track from Canadian singer-songwriter Fefe Dobson 's official sophomore studio album , Joy .
Dobson penned the song with Andrea Wasse , who is the vocalist of Ontario Pop-Rock outfit The Weekend , along with American guitarist David Lichens .
Dobson told Telus about the recording of the song : `` It was a fun song to do because it really is about feeling confident ... Not bring afraid to be a complete woman , entering a room and saying : ` Look at me move , fight and follow my own dreams . '
It 's about trust , internal and external . ''
