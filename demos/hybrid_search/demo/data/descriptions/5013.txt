This Salaam Remi produced track is about a late-night street encounter in Queens during the '90s .
Nas tells us of such real-life gangsters as Colbert `` Black Just '' Johnson , E Moneybags and Stretch Walker who met their maker in Nas ' neck of the woods .
The Queens rapper goes on to celebrate the perseverance and achievements of the survivors who are still around .
Remi and Nas had had the idea for this track for several years before finally cutting the tune .
