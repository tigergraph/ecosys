This song was composed by R&B musician R Kelly and produced and sung by Maxwell .
The song is Maxwell 's biggest hit to date , peaking at # 4 on the Hot 100 and topping the US R&B chart for eight weeks .
It also spent 18 weeks at # 1 on the R&B airplay chart , the longest-leading chart-topper in that tally 's history .
This was included on the soundtrack of the Eddie Murphy comedy Life .
This won Best R&B / Soul Single -LRB- Male -RRB- at the Soul Train Awards and Best R&B Single of the Year at the Billboard Music Awards .
