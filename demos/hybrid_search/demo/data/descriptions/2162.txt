BC Jean is an American singer-songwriter and Pop/Rock singer .
After writing and recording the first version of Beyoncé 's mega hit `` If I Were a Boy , '' the San Diego native was the subject of a signing frenzy .
Jean eventually signed with J Records and released `` Just a Guy '' on September 14 , 2010 .
The song was produced by OneRepublic frontman Ryan Tedder .
His other credits include Leona Lewis ' `` Bleeding Love '' and Beyoncé 's `` Halo . ''
Tedder said : `` This is the first song I 've ever produced that I did n't write in six years ... That 's how much I like this song .
I have a good feeling about this one . ''
