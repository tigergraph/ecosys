American Christian Rock band Red close their fourth studio album Release The Panic with this track in which they celebrate being a new creation in Christ .
Guitarist Anthony Armstrong explained the song 's meaning to Artist Direct .
`` We say goodbye to our previous life and look forward to new beginnings , '' he said .
`` This is the anthem on the record .
We share in this feeling with all those that choose to start over .
The chorus of this song literally made this song what it is -- uplifting . ''
