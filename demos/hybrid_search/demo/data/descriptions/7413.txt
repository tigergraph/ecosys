Despite only reaching # 24 in the ARIA singles chart , this feel good dance tune is the longest charting single in Australian history at 73 weeks .
The record was previously held by The Living End 's `` Second Solution/Prisoner of Society , '' which spent 69 weeks on the listing .
Vocalist Connie Mitchell is best known outside of Australia for featuring on four tracks on Kanye West 's 2007 album Gradustion , including the singles `` Flashing Lights , '' and `` Ca n't Tell Me Nothing . ''
