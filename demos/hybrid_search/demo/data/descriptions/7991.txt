Hicks told the Birmingham News about this Blues-Rock number : `` Traveling to the west side of Mississippi in a conversion van with no air-conditioning in the middle of summer .
That was the inspiration , and I have first-hand experience .
-LRB- Long-time Clapton collaborator -RRB- Doyle -LRB- Bramhall II -RRB- plays a great slide guitar part in it . ''
This is a cover of a song that was originally the title track of a 2002 album by Alabama indie band Spoonful James .
Hicks penned it with Spoonful James guitarist Wynn Christian , who is a close friend of the American Idol winner .
