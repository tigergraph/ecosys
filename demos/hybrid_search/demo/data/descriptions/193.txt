The title track of The Mars Volta 's sixth album comes from a device in vocalist Cedric Bixler-Zavala 's imagination .
During a concert he explained that the fictional contraption would take a person 's problems and help them overcome them , `` stopping the night from coming , leaving nothing but light . ''
Noctourniquet is a concept album centering on a character based on the mythical Greek figure Hyacinthus and the English nursery rhyme Solomon Grundy .
