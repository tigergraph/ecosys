This is a pre-single track from Australian singer-songwriter Natalie Imbruglia 's fourth studio album , Come to Life .
It was released digitally prior to the physical release of the first official single , `` Want . ''
The song 's music video was directed by Mike Baldwin , who previously helmed the clip for Gabriella Climi 's `` Sweet About Me . ''
English comedians Alan Carr and David Walliams make cameo appearances as do Sam and Holly Branson , the son and daughter of Virgin mogul , Richard.Branson .
