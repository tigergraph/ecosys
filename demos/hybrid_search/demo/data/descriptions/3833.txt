This song was performed by Coral drummer Ian Skelly and guitarist Lee Southall with the latter on vocals .
It is a previously unreleased track recorded in Skelly 's bedroom in around 2000 .
Frontman James Skelley commented on his record label 's website : `` Lee is a great singer , he should sing more but he shirks it sometimes . ''
He added : `` We just forgot about this song and only found it when we were looking for demos for this collection . ''
