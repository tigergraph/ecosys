This is the second single from Puerto-Rican singer Ricky Martin 's ninth studio and first bilingual album Musica + Alma + Sexo .
It was released on December 21 , 2010 .
Martin told Billboard magazine about the song : `` The lyrics to this song changed a lot .
And we go back to the same point .
I needed to make the lyrics my own .
It 's a goodbye .
You 're leaving , but we 're still friends . ''
A Spanish-language version is also included on the album titled `` Te Vas . ''
