This bluegrass-flavored song is a duet with Ronnie McCoury of the Del McCoury Band .
Bentley has recorded at least one bluegrass song on every album he has recorded to date .
He told AOL : `` I would n't have a career without bluegrass music .
There 's a bar in Nashville called the Station Inn - that 's where I got my start .
So it really has a special place in my heart . ''
McCoury originally wrote this song with the title `` Last Call For Alcohol . ''
It is the second McCoury song Bentley has recorded .
He previously covered `` Good Man Like Me '' on his 2005 Modern Day Drifter set .
