Weezer frontman Rivers Cuomo described this tune to the NME as `` mid '90s emotional rock . ''
The song was co-written by Ryan Adams , who also contributes guitar and bass .
Cuomo told MTV News about their collaboration .
`` It 's a very stirring and emotional and powerful Weezer song .
He was really inspiring to work with .
He has a lot of wild and crazy energy . ''
