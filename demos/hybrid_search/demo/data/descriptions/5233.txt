Darwin Deez mainman Darwin Smith described this tune to NME as being `` a song about hope and despair in the modern world . ''
He explained that it is `` an homage to my 6 years '' at a vegan restaurant called Angelica Kitchen in NYC where he worked as an expeditor and a server.Smith added : `` I am a dreamer and I live through fantasy .
Sometimes I feel like I 'm not getting anywhere in life and on top of that my heart is broken or breaking but I never stop believing that happiness is possible and that happiness is easy if you can figure it out . ''
