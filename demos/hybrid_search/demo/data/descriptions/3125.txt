This song was co-written by English singer-songwriter Lucie Silvas , who has had some chart success under her own name scoring two UK Top Ten hits in the mid 2000s with `` What You 're Made Of '' and `` Breathe In . ''
Also check out on this site her album track `` Last Man Standing . ''
The other co-writer was another English singer-songwriter Gary Go , who has his own Top 40 hit in the UK in 2009 with `` Wonderful . ''
