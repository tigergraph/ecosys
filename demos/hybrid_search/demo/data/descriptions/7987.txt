One of the themes of Artwork is the concept of mortality .
Frontman and songwriter Bert McCracken told Alternative Press that having previously lived a `` reckless life , '' he came to terms with how easy it is to accidentally die after the tragic passing of Heath Ledger .
This song is loosely based on the death of the Australian actor and not being able to say `` sorry , '' or `` I never meant to die '' after an accidental death .
