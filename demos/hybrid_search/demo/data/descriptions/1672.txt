Willy Moon grew up in Wellington , New Zealand .
He moved to London at the age of 18 and began making songs with Pro Tools on his personal computer as he did n't yet know any other musicians .
In 2010 , Moon released this song on Myspace , which got him noticed and he subsequently acquired a record deal with Island Records .
It is the oldest track to appear on his debut album , Here 's Willy Moon .
