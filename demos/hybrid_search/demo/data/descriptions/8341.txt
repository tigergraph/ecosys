On this murderous epic , Peter Buck unleashes a torrent of vicious psychedelic feedback .
Frontman Gary Lightbody told Uncut magazine about the R.E.M. guitarist 's contribution to the album .
`` He 's all over every song , sometimes playing two or three instruments .
He kept on surprising us - playing mandolin as well as he does , to giving us that wall of feedback on the last song on the record .
It was a constant surprise . ''
