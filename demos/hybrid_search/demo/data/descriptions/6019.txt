This is the second single by American R&B singer Monica , from her sixth studio album , titled Still Standing .
The song was written by Crystal Johnson , Bryan-Michael Cox and Jermaine Dupri .
Love did indeed come all over Monica as four months after filming the video she and Shannon Brown announced their engagement via Twitter .
The couple was married in a secret ceremony at their Los Angeles home on November 22 , 2010 .
The Chris Robinson directed music video features NBA athlete Shannon Brown and `` Hi Hater '' rapper Maino .
Said Monica : `` This video truly speaks volumes to something I strongly believe in .
And that 's following your heart . ''
