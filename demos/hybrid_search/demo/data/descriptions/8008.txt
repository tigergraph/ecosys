This was the first single for Nas .
He recorded it for the soundtrack to the 1992 film Zebrahead , and the song was included on his debut album Illmatic in 1994 .
This samples from `` Schoolboy Crush '' by Average White Band , `` Hawaiian Sophie '' by Jay-Z , `` Soul Traveling '' by Gary Byrd and `` Dead End '' by Japanese Hair .
