This song features Clapton 's former flame Sheryl Crow on shared lead vocals .
The pair had a brief affair in the late 1990s , and it is rumored she wrote her song `` My Favorite Mistake '' about him .
Clapton previously guested with the American singer at 1999 's Sheryl Crow And Friends : Live From Central Park concert and the 2007 Crossroads Guitar Festival .
Texan guitarist and member of Clapton 's band Doyle Bramhall II wrote the song with former Mark Ronson/Beck collaborator Justin Stanley and his wife US R&B singer-songwriter Nikka Costa .
