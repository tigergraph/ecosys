This was released as the second single from Pop Evil 's third album , Onyx .
It acted as the follow-up to their first # 1 on the active rock radio chart , `` Trenches . ''
The song 's music video is part two of a three part video trilogy that revolves around addiction and abuse .
It goes in reverse order , with `` Trenches '' being the final installment and the opening part featuring the song `` Behind Closed Doors . ''
Drummer Chachi Riot said about the song : `` Life can be wonderful , but life can also be extremely merciless .
How do we deal with these hard times ?
Who do we have to deal with ?
Our decisions have consequences . ''
