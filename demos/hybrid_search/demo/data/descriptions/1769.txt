The subject matter of this song is a fantasy of what a casual relationship might become .
The song samples `` One More Chance '' by The Notorious B.I.G. On this R&B jam Carey pokes fun at her own vocal signature with the lyric , `` love me down till I hit the top of my soprano . ''
In an interview with Elle Magazine , Carey commented : `` The album has a sense of humor-you can hear me laughing out loud at certain points . ''
