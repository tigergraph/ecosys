This samples the Greg Perry track `` Come on Down -LRB- Get Your Head Out of the Clouds -RRB- '' from his 1975 album One for the Road .
This track was produced by J.U.S.T.I.C.E. League , which stands for `` Just Undeniably Some of The Illest Composers Ever . ''
Among the other production credits this Florida based group of hip hop producers have on their CV are `` She Got It '' for 2 Pistols and `` No One Will Do '' from Mary J Blige 's 2005 Grammy winning album The Breakthrough .
