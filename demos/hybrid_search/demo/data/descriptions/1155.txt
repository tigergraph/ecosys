Morning Parade are an alternative rock band from Harlow , Essex , comprising vocalist/guitarist Steve Sparrow , bassist Phill Titus , guitarist Chad Thomas , keyboardist Ben Giddings , and drummer Andrew Hayes .
The band was formed in 2007 and after signing to Parlophone a few years later , they released this song as a demo for free download on morningparade.com in Spring 2010 .
Sparrow told The Sun : `` A&E is a song about feeling paranoid about infidelity when we were all having women trouble . ''
The song title is an abbreviation for Accident and Emergency , which in the UK is a common name for the emergency department of a hospital .
