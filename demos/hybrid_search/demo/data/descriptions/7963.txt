This track from Dev 's debut studio album , The Night the Sun Came Up finds the Electro-Pop singer telling her lover she wants to be kissed rather than touched up .
`` Get you hand off my hip/And kiss my lips , kiss my lips , kiss me all over / Are you gon na take that risk , take that risk , 'cause only getting older , '' she croons .
Dev penned the song with her usual collaborators , the LA Valley electro-pop duo The Cataracs .
A remixed version featuring rapper Fabolous was released to Urban radio .
