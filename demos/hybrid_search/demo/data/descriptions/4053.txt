This Motown pastiche was written and produced by the Norwegian duo of Hitesh Ceon and Kim Ofstad , who work under the name of ELEMENT .
The twosome also co-wrote and produced `` Dangerous , '' the B-side of Burke 's UK chart-topper `` Bad Boys . ''
They were previously known as 3Elementz , which was a 3 member team , including bass-player Jonny Sjo .
3Elementz were best known for their production work on Madcon 's 2008 European hit `` Beggin . ''
