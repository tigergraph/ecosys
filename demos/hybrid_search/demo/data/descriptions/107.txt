This is the second single by R&B singer-songwriter and member of the Odd Future collective Frank Ocean .
The song is taken from his EP Nostalga , Lite , a re-release of his Nostalga , Ultra mixtape .
The single was released on August 16 , 2011 .
Lyrically , the song finds Ocean driving to a funeral in his Lincoln Towncar , which we later find out is to be his own .
The song features production from The-Dream 's right-hand man , Christopher `` Tricky '' Stewart , the guy behind the hit singles `` Single Ladies -LRB- Put a Ring on it -RRB- '' and `` Umbrella . ''
