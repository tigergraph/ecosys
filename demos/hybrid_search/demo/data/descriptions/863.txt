This is the second single from Rock group Them Crooked Vultures self-titled debut album .
The song climaxes with a totally unexpected brass band outro .
The song features guitarist Josh Homme and drummer Dave Grohl.trading vocals over a stop-start riff , which a number of critics have compared to The Knack 's `` My Sharona . ''
It is the only track on the album that Grohl contributes vocals to .
