This was released as the first single from August Burns Red 's Rescue & Restore .
The album was made available for pre-order on iTunes on June 4 , 2013 with a free download of this song .
The song is written from the perspective of a soldier serving overseas trying to hold on to hope .
Guitarist and principal songwriter JB Brubaker explained to The Alternative Press : `` The lyrics are about being stuck in a depressing time/place in your life but trying to remember there is a light at the end of the tunnel . ''
The song 's interlude features a spoken word part as frontman Jake Luhrs reads a letter .
