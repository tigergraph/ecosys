Part of the song goes : Oh Havana I 've been searching for you everywhereAnd though I 'll never be thereI know what I would see thereI can always find my Cuban skiesIn Rosalinda 's eyesIronically , Billy Joel would actually get to visit Havana less than 6 months later .
In early March 1979 , he performed in Cuba at the Karl Marx Theater , along with Kris Kristofferson , Weather Report , RitaCoolidge , and Billy Swan .
The three day music festival was known as Havana Jam .
