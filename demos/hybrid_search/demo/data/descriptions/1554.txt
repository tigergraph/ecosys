This piano led piece is an iTunes Pre-Order bonus track on San Francisco garage rockers Black Rebel Motorcycle Club 's sixth studio album , Beat The Devil 's Tattoo .
The song is an adaptation of Edgar Allan Poe 's 1839 story of everlasting love beyond the grave .
Earlier in 2010 North Carolina post hardcore band Alesana released a concept album based on the same Poe short story -LRB- check out `` The Thespian '' for details -RRB- .
Beat The Devil 's Tattoo is a phrase taken from another Edgar Allan Poe short story , The Devil In The Belfry .
