This look inside the daily grind of a couple resigned to staying together was written by the three Annies in June 2012 while they sat in hair and makeup , prepping for a performance at CMA Music Festival .
It features the line , `` Hey , hey , it 's alright , everybody fusses , everybody fights . ''
Angaleena Presley told Billboard magazine : `` That 's the message of that song , ` it might suck , but we 're stayin ' together , by god . ' ''
