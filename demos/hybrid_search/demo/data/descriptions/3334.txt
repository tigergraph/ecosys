This is the closing track of Russian American singer/songwriter Nika Roza Danilova - aka Zola Jesus ' - third studio album , Conatus .
The LP title is a term that refers to the momentum to keep evolving .
Danilova explained this song 's meaning to NME : `` It 's saying that once the audience goes away I have nothing else .
People are like ` Oh that 's so beautiful , you 're so histrionic . '
But I 'm like : this is all I have , man .
I do n't have anything else .
And the day you guys stop listening ... well , what am I going to do ? ''
