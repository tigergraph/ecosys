This song was written and performed by Fiona Apple , for the Judd Apatow directed movie , This Is 40 .
Apple 's frequent collaborator , Jon Brion , produced this track as well as penning parts of the score for the film .
It was released as the lead single from the soundtrack on November 17 , 2012 .
This was the first song ever to be written by Apple for a movie soundtrack .
