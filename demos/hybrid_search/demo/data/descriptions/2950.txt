This ska-tinged track features Santigold .
The story of how the UK dance/electronic duo Basement Jaxx hooked up with the American singer-songwriter goes as follows .
The twosome were in New York to record a track with Yoko Ono for their 5th studio album , Scars .
Unfortunately Felix Buxton from the duo had a bust-up with his girlfriend , who was upset at the time he was spending in the Big Apple .
A fuming Buxton penned this track after their row .
He recalled to The London Times September 18 , 2009 `` To get it off my chest I sang it when I got into the studio and Santigold came in and said : ` That 's wicked !
I 'd like to sing that ' . ''
