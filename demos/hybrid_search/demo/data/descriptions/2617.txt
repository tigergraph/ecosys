Avenged Sevenfold wrote this song about the American troops who were fighting the war in Iraq .
The band claims to have friends who fought in the war , and wanted to give their listeners an idea of what war is like .
Even though they are not a political band , they wanted to present the view of someone going to battle as a troop .
