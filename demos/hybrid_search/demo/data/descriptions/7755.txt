In this heartache song , Nalick sings about being on the verge of giving up on love .
She is trying to leave the past behind and struggles to quiet the thoughts in her head .
Most of Nalick 's lyrics come from entries in her journal .
In 2006 , Nalick recorded a new version of this song with producer Steve Lillywhite .
