Jay Sean recorded this song for a deluxe re-release of his 2008 album My Own Way .
A re-mixed version was recorded featuring Lil Wayne , in order to launch the British/Asian singer-songwriter stateside .
This was co-written by Claude Kelly whose other songwriting credits include Kelly Clarkson 's `` My Life Would Suck Without You '' and Britney Spears ' `` Circus . ''
