This was the first track written by British alternative punk band The Horrors for their third album , Skying .
Bassist Rhys Webb told NME that he and vocalist Faris Badwan started work on it , then sent it to synth player Tom Cowan , who introduced the break and brass at the end . ''
He added : `` After a year on the road , before the night was out we had recorded the first demo .
It was so relaxed and pretty exciting .
I left saying to myself , ` If that 's the first track we 've written , what 's the rest of it going to sound like ? '
`` The song was released as the album 's first single and premiered on May 24 , 2011 on Zane Lowe 's BBC Radio 1 show .
