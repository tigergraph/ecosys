Iron Man and Game of Thrones composer Ramin Djawadi created the soundtrack for the Guillermo del Toro directed robots versus aliens movie , Pacific Rim .
Djawadi linked up with Rage Against the Machine and Audioslave guitarist Tom Morello for the film 's theme .
`` Guillermo and I were discussing adding a rock element to the score and Tom 's unique style and sounds really defined our robots , '' Djawadi told Rolling Stone .
Morello told Rolling Stone that the collaboration was a no-brainer .
`` When they asked me to put some giant robot riffs and screaming underwater monster licks on the film score , I was all in , '' he said .
