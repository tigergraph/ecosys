This was released as a double-A sided solo single by Radiohead frontman Thom Yorke along with `` FeelingPulledApartbyHorses . ''
Radiohead 's long-time collaborator Nigel Godrich produced this track .
Yorke explained on Radiohead 's online blog Dead Air Space that this is a `` bass menace that was born out of the -LSB- 2006 solo album -RSB- The Eraser period , but needed a little more time . ''
The song 's music video , which was directed by media artist and experimental filmmaker Raymond Salvatore Harmon , utilizes imagery by quasi-anonymous graffiti artist Banksy superimposed over various time lapse shots of London city life .
