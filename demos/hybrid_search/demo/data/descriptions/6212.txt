Jordan was asked by the Observer Music Monthly October 14 , 2007 , who he is referring to in the line `` I 've learned from teachers that could orchestrate the whole wide world ? ''
He replied : '' ` Jill Sinclair and Trevor Horn .
Every time I sing it I remember how Jill used to be .
She was so confident , so sure .
I want to be like her when I 'm older . ''
Jill Sinclair is producer Trevor Horn 's wife .
In the middle of the recording of the album tragedy struck when Horn and Sinclair 's 22-year-old son Aaron accidentally shot his mother in the neck with his air rifle , leaving her in a coma .
