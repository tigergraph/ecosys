This features and was produced by Will.i.am from the Black Eyed Peas .
The song 's backdrop and beat are sampled from Pretty Tony 's freestyle/electro track `` Jam the Box , '' from his 1984 album Fix it in the Mix .
Ciara sampled the same track on `` That 's Right '' from her 2006 Ciara : The Evolution album .
The Mexican and Puerto Rican singer Tiffany Villarreal provides the background vocals on this track .
