This was the first track to be released by the English synthpop duo Hurts , from their sophomore album Exile .
It received its first worldwide radio play on the BBC Radio 1 Breakfast show on January 4 , 2013 .
The lyrics are about a car accident and were inspired by JG Ballard 's novel Crash and colored by Cormac McCarthy 's apocalyptic book The Road , which Hutchcraft read three times while writing Exile .
