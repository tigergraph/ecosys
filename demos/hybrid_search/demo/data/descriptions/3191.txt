Though this arena rocker is a personal , relationship-based song , all the metaphors Matt Bellamy uses relate to things that he read about concerning the second law of thermodynamics .
The law states that because energy is naturally expended and consumed and no new energy produced , unlimited growth is unsustainable .
Bellamy told Q magazine that on this song he is , `` talking about cooling down , fighting for love , all that sort of stuff . ''
