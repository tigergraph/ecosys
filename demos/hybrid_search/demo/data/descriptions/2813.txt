The second single from Ghanaian British rapper Tinchy Stryder 's fourth studio album finds him enjoying female company in Las Vegas .
The track was produced by Scottish DJ Calvin Harris and released on November 6 , 2011 .
The music video was co-directed by Luke Monaghan and features Tinchy taking on Vegas with his groupies .
His female companions are all clothed in the Ruff Squad member 's Star In The Hood clothing brand .
