In 1989 , The Rolling Stones went to Tangier , Morocco for 3 days to record this song with the Master Musicians of Jajouka led by Bachir Attar for the Stones ' album Steel Wheels .
After the recording sessions , Mick Jagger visited the village of Jajouka , which Brian Jones had visited in 1968 .
This song marked a return to the world music The Stones experimented with in the '60s .
The squeal at the beginning is Keith Richards scraping a knife against a bicycle wheel .
