This song is the first single from American rapper Gucci Mane 's sixth album .
It originally featured on his mixtape , Writing on the Wall .
An official remix of the song featuring rapper OJ Da Juiceman appeared on Gucci Mane 's EP Wasted : The Prequel .
This celebration of a party lifestyle features rapper Plies .
The two hip hop artists also penned this track , which was produced by Fatboi .
