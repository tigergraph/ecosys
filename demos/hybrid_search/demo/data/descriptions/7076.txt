In this song Buckingham looks back at the decadent times he spent with Fleetwood Mac and contrasts it with the relatively calm life he is living now .
He explained to The London Times September 13 , 2008 : `` I lived in Bel Air for a number of years as a bachelor with some crazy girlfriends .
I also built a house there when I started my family .
Fame is really funny , it gives you lots of freedom but then at the same time it takes away a lot of yourself . ''
