The band said of this tribute to New Orleans on their MySpace site : `` We felt it very important to show our adoration for one of our favorite cities and one of the most culturally rich and vibrant places in the world . ''
Guitarist Lars Frederiksen is the lead vocalist on this song .
Bassist Matt Freeman said : `` I feel the bass line is a tribute to -LRB- Booker T. & the M.G.s bass guitarist -RRB- Donald ` Duck ' Dunn .
It has a lot of his style .
New Orleans is an important city to any musician that has ever been there .
We have been lucky enough to have been going there since the '80s with Operation Ivy .
I know it will come back .
It 's that special and resilient a place . ''
