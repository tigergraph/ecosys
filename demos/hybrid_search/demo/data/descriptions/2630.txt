This Leon Russell song was recorded by Donny Hathaway on his self-titled 1971 album .
Leon Jackson explained to the Daily Record October 10 , 2008 , why he covered it on Right Now : `` I 've always loved this song .
I get to showcase my voice and it 'll be a beautiful one to sing live . ''
American Idol contestant Elliott Yamin sang this on the show .
Simon Cowell referred to the performance as a `` vocal masterclass . ''
