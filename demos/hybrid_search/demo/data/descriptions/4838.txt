The middle part of this song samples Mozart 's `` The String Quartet No. 21 in D Major , '' which is nicknamed `` The Violet . ''
The quartet was written for and dedicated to the King of Prussia , Friedrich Wilhelm II , who was an amateur cellist .
Mozart and his friend Karl Lichnowsky met the king in Potsdam in April 1789 and played before the king the following month in Berlin .
The vocalist is Ssegawa-Ssekintu Kiwanuka .
Born to Ugandan parents , and schooled in Croydon , the sometime Clean Bandit frontman received a Full Blue in Boxing , the highest sporting honour at Cambridge University where the group met .
His CV also includes being made the youngest-ever nominee at the global Institution of Chemical Engineers Awards .
