This was originally written by Grace Jones with the English Trip-Hop artist Tricky in 1997 for an album that was never completed .
It was released as a White Label 12-inch single .
Ten years later Jones re-recorded the song as the title track of her 2008 album .
Hurricane was Jones ' first studio album release for 19 years .
Her previous offering , Bulletproof Heart , came out in 1989 .
