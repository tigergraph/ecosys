Campbell penned this track from his self-titled debut album with Dan Couch , whose other credits include co-writing Kip Moore 's breakthrough single `` Somethin' ` Bout a Truck . ''
When the singer performed this song at CMT 's Rock A Sold-Out Benefit Show on March 2 , 2012 he explained to the audience it describes how he thought that his first baby with his wife Mindy was going to be a boy .
She turned out to be a beautiful little girl .
