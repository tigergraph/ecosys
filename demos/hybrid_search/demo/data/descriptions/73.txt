Drums frontman Jonathan Pierce 's parents are strictly religious , but the singer decided not to follow their faith and this song touches on his break from Christianity .
He told NME : `` When you 're a kid and you 're just learning about the world you 're born into , it 's just an animal thing to look to your parents for their take on the world , at least until a certain age .
So yeah , I guess I thought I was religious , but looking now I know it was all bull s -- t. '' About the album title : `` Portamento '' is a musical term that refers to a vocal slide between two pitches .
