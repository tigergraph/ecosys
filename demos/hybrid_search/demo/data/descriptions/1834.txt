This is the second single to be released by London-based musical duo The 2 Bears .
It finds the duo singing , `` We 've got ta work/work harder for each other/for the future . ''
Joe Goddard of The 2 Bears told The Sun that the lyrics describe how he aims to lives his life , now that he 's a father .
`` I had a baby girl in 2010 so I felt I wanted to provide for her , '' he explained .
`` I wanted to work hard at what I do , which is make music .
It 's a privileged thing to do so I put everything into it . ''
The song was made available on January 1 , 2012 as a Digital download in the United Kingdom .
