This was written by Morrissey and guitarist Boz Boorer .
Boorer has collaborated with Morrissey on a number of other singles including the 1994 UK Top Ten hit `` The More You Ignore Me , The Closer I Get . ''
American Singer - songwriter Kristeen Young supplied the operatic background vocals .
Young opened for Morrissey during his 2006-07 `` Ringleader of the Tormentors '' tour .
