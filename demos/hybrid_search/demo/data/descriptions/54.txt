According to promotional materials , this collaboration with R&B singer Ciara is inspired by `` love and loss . ''
Another version of this upbeat dance track was recorded featuring German singer Sarah Connor rather than Ciara .
This was released in some European countries including Germany and Poland .
This was produced by RedOne who also co-wrote the song with songwriter Frankie Storm and Iglesias .
RedOne is best known for his work with Lady Gaga -LRB- `` Just Dance , '' `` Poker Face '' -RRB- .
Iglesias debuted the song on the French TV show Star Academy .
He performed it as a duet with one of the contestants , Gaultier .
The performance propelled the song into the Top 3 of the French singles chart .
