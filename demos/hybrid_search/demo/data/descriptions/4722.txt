This song is a tribute to Andrew Wood , lead singer of the Seattle grunge band Mother Love Bone .
Wood died of a heroin overdose in 1990 , and his band mates Jeff Ament and Stone Gossard joined Chris Cornell and Matt Cameron from Soundgarden to form Temple Of The Dog and release one album in honor of Wood .
Cornell wrote the song .
