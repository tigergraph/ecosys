This song was originally recorded by Cher and released as a single in 1979 .
It became the American singer 's first Top Ten single in her home country in five years , peaking at # 8 .
This was the first solo release for Ellis-Bextor , who had previously enjoyed some success as the voice of TheAudience and Spiller .
She is also the daughter of former Blue Peter presenter Janet Ellis .
In 2001 Sophie Ellis-Bextor released a slightly re-written version as the lead single from her Read My Lips album .
It was an instant hit , going straight to # 2 in the UK Singles Chart .
Cher however complained about the raunchy new lyrics .
