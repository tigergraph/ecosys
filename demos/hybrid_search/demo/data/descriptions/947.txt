Djbooth.net asked Budden which song on Padded Room , does he consider , best exemplifies his overall growth as an emcee since his debut set .
The rapper replied that this tune about dreams and nightmares does , because creatively it 's , `` on a whole different level . ''
He added : `` I could name some other records where the rhyme schemes and the wordplay and the delivery and everything is more complex , but I think , for Joe Budden fans , and for Joe Budden , I like to focus on the creative side , and havin ' a thought and bein ' able to execute it in the booth .
I think that song best displays me being able to do that . ''
