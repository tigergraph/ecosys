This is the lead single and title track from Latino rap group Cypress Hill 's eighth studio album .
It was their first studio release for six years , their previous record being 2004 's Till Death Do Us Part .
The album was recorded over the course of three years at lead rapper B-Real 's studio , The Temple , in Los Angeles .
The song was co-written and co-produced by Rage Against The Machine 's Tom Morello .
The guitarist also contributed to a second track , `` Shut 'Em Down . ''
`` It was great to work with my longtime friends and comrades Cypress Hill on these two monster rock tracks , '' said Morello .
