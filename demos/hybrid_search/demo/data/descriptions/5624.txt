This final part of the opening four-part suite on Once I Was an Eagle finds Marling finally leaving her lover after their tempestuous relationship .
`` How cruel you were to me .
How cruel time can be , '' she croons .
The song got its first live debut on Laura Marling 's North-American tour in June 2012 .
Ruth de Turberville 's cello traces the line of the song 's melody .
The cellist and Laura became friends at Leighton Park School , a private Quaker school in Reading , Berkshire .
They have since toured together and Ruth has contributed to a number of Laura 's songs .
She also provided cello and backing vocals on Ellie Goulding 's hit single , `` Your Song . ''
