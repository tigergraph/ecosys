Like `` 2 +2 = 5 , '' this song was inspired by Thom Yorke 's compulsive tracking of BBC Radio 4 news bulletins .
This track was particularly inspired by his habit of walking in the countryside with a portable radio .
According to Q magazine April 2008 , the lyric about `` Yesterday 's headlines blown by the wind '' was derived from a passage in Thomas Pynchon 's 1963 novel V , about the search for a mysterious vanished woman . ''
