This song about extra-terrestrials was written by Newton Faulkner and his brother Toby .
Newton Faulkner in The Daily Mail September 14 , 2007 said : `` Writing with my brother is always great fun .
We used to make up songs on the way back from the pub .
With this one , Toby even came up with a dance for the chorus . ''
