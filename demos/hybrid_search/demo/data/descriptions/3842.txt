The son of two Church of England vicars , as a boy Tom McRae sang in the church choir .
Even though he has not pursued his parents ' faith , the singer-songwriter retains fond memories of attending midnight mass on Christmas Eve .
He explained : `` Even an atheist can have fun singing carols and being drunk at midnight mass .
At least me and the priest had that much in common .
This might be the happiest song I 've ever written , which makes a change . ''
