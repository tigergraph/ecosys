Vamps is a Japanese Rock duo formed in 2008 by vocalist Hyde and guitarist K.A.Z This track is included on their best-of album Sex Blood Rock n ' Roll , which contains re-recordings of their songs , all in English .
It was their first worldwide release .
Hyde explained the song 's meaning to Artist Direct : `` I 've always found human folly intriguing , and I wanted to point it out from a vampire 's perspective , '' he said .
`` I thought it would convey better from a vampire 's point of view , so I tried to express humans through the eyes of a vampire . ''
