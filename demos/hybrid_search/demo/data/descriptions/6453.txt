This tune about her hometown of Toledo in Ohio was written by 2010 American Idol runner up , Crystal Bowersox , before her tenure on the program .
The song was played on the May 19 , 2009 Top 3 Results show as the background music for her homecoming package , when Bowersox returned to Toledo .
It was the first ever instance of a contestant 's own song being performed almost in its entirety on Idol .
