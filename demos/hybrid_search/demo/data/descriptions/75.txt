Mike Rosenberg is a British singer-songwriter and indie Pop/Folk/Rock singer who records under the moniker of Passenger .
This is the opening track of his third album , All The Little Lights .
Rosenberg explained to VH1 : `` It 's a song about how difficult it is to remain positive and keep on pursuing what you love .
You have to be grateful for what you have , and not always striving towards what 's over there . ''
