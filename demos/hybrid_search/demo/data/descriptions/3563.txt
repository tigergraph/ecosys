Former 4 Non Blondes member Linda Perry , who has penned many hit singles , including Christina Aguilera 's `` Beautiful , '' was the writer and producer of this sweeping ballad .
4 Non Blondes became the first lesbian Rock band to have a mainstream hit when `` What 's Up '' scored in 1992 .
By 2009 , an artist 's sexual preference was far less of an issue , and Lambert came out as gay shortly after finishing second in the American Idol competition .
