Wale references Kemba Walker of the Charlotte Bobcats when he spits , `` I ` ma let the chips fall/Niggas is Kemba Walker , tryna see me pitfall . ''
The lyric alludes to the NBA baller 's ability to knock defenders off their feet .
Walker is a fledging hip-hop artist himself .
On June 21 , 2011 , he released a mixtape in collaboration with DJ Skee , EZ Pass , which features songs inspired by and inspiring to him .
