On this song , West sings from the perspective of a woman breaking up with her man .
Like most of the other tracks on 808s & Heartbreak this was very much influenced by the ending of his 18-month engagement to the designer Alexis Phifer .
West created the piano arrangement himself .
During an album listening session , West told the audience that this track is his favorite from the album .
The Chicago artist added that when making this track , he was in his zone , like Jay-Z would be .
