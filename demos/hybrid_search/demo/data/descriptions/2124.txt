This is a bonus track on the deluxe edition of Raditude .
Rhythm guitarist Brian Bell told MTV News that this Garrett `` Jacknife '' Lee produced number , is one of his favorite tracks on the album .
He explained that the producer encouraged him to play his guitar almost like a violin .
`` He had me picking really fast high up on the neck in what I call ` butterfly picking , ' '' he said .
`` He would plug anything into anything to get something interesting , even if it sounded crappy . ''
