The Rubik 's Cube is a 3-D mechanical puzzle invented in 1974 by Hungarian sculptor and professor of architecture Erno Rubik .
It is widely considered to be the world 's best-selling toy .
There are 43,252,003,274,489,856,000 different color combinations possible on a Rubik 's Cube .
The World Rubik Cube championship was held in Budapest on June 5 , 1982 .
Nineteen National Champions took part .
Minh Thai , the US Champion , won by solving the Cube in 22.95 seconds .
The world record , in competitive conditions , has grown progressively lower and now stands at 7.08 seconds .
It was set by Erik Akkersdiik at the Czech Open 2008 .
