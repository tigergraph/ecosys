Mavis Staples began her gospel career with The Staples Family Singers way back in 1950 before she 'd even entered her teens .
Now in her eighth decade , she 's still recording and entering new territory .
Remarkably this song , according to Mavis , is her first-ever a cappella recording .
You Are Not Alone became the highest-ranking album of Staples ' career when it debuted at # 69 on the Billboard 200 .
You Are Not Alone was produced by Jeff Tweedy , the singer/songwriter who fronts indie rock band Wilco .
