In this 10 minute album closer , Callahan sings `` It 's time to put God away . ''
Callahan explained to Mojo magazine April 2009 : `` I 've been reading a lot of atheist writers : Mark Twain , Richard Dawkins , Robert Ingersoll , Ian McEwan , Mencken , Lovecraft .
Obama mentioned ` non-believers , ' in his inauguration speech .
I wanted to write a song that backed all this up . ''
