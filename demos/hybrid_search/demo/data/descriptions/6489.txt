Hombre Lobo is a concept album about a werewolf who experiences various types of desire .
The anti-hero acts in different ways , for instance in this song he is gentle but in `` Fresh Blood '' he is menacing .
Eels ' main man Mark Oliver Everett explained to The Sun June 5 , 2009 : `` They 're different attempts at making a sales pitch from this -LRB- werewolf -RRB- character to the object of his desire .
He 's trying to convince her that he 's THE guy .
Sometimes he takes a more tender approach , sometimes he gets frustrated and loses his cool and things get a little ugly . ''
