Sullivan told The Sun January 2 , 2009 that this song `` is a plea to women to leave abusive relationships . ''
Sullivan expanded on this powerful tale of an abusive relationship to the Daily Mirror March 27 , 2009 : `` It 's an experience people have and it was a song I hoped would give them strength .
It happens everyday .
I hope that song helps them get out of that situation before it really gets out of hand . ''
Hip Hop producer Salaam Remi produced this track .
This tune samples Remi 's own song `` Police Theme . ''
