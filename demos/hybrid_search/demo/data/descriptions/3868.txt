This song that describes the images of nightlife in New York City was written and produced by Lauper together with Swedish producers Peer Åström and Johan Bobäck .
Swedish hit maker Max Martin also has a song-writing credit on this .
Lauper also collaborated with Swedish producer Kleerup on the album .
She explained to Rolling Stone magazine that she checked out the Swedish music scene because `` the Swedes have this incredible melody sense . ''
Lauper explained to Entertainment Weekly that this song came from `` from the title of the Henry Miller book Into the Night Life , which inspired Lawrence Ferlinghetti 's -LRB- 1958 collection of poems -RRB- A Convey Island of the Mind , which inspired me to describe the images of nightlife here in NYC . ''
