This Stargate-produced track is the third single from American dance-pop singer Alexis Jordan 's self-titled debut album .
The song was written by the New York based singer/songwriter Autumn Rowe , who also composed the singer 's debut single , `` Happiness '' and by the French producer Sandy Vee , who previously co-penned Alexis 's second single , `` Good Girl . ''
Clifton Bell , who has previously worked with Trey Songz , directed the video .
It sees Alexis participate in a break dancing style dance-off .
