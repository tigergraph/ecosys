This song was written by singer/songwriter Teedra Moses .
Teedra worked on the song with producer Paul Poli ; the two recorded many songs for Teedra 's debut album .
`` Dip it Low '' was intended for Teedra 's debut album , Complex Simplicity , but her version became the demo that was accepted by Christina Milian .
