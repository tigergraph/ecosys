This song about living in the moment was Jerrod Niemann 's fourth single release on Sea Gayle , a sister label of Arista Nashville , as well as his seventh overall .
Niemann penned the song with Rob Hatch , Lee Brice and Lance Miller .
The song 's visual was filmed at the East Nashville restaurant and music venue , Family Wash. `` The video for ` Shinin ' On Me ' is meant to show you that you do n't have to let the storms of life ruin your day , '' Niemann explained to The Boot .
