This song features vocals by Becky Hill , who first came to the British public 's attention when she reached the semifinals of the the first series of The Voice before being eliminated .
Hill provided cover for Ella Eyre 's vocals on `` Waiting All Night '' during Rudimental 's set at Glastonbury 2013 and Bestival 2013 and also was the singer on Wilkinson 's hit tune `` Afterglow , '' the same year .
