Kevin Jonas said of this pop/dance anthem in Entertainment Weekly : `` The chorus goes , ` You 're gon na be , be good to me / 'Cause I 'm gon na be , be good to you .
It 's a big sing-along song .
And it 's fun . ''
This was co-written by John Taylor who helps produce for the brothers as well as playing lead guitar .
Taylor also had a songwriting credit on `` Games '' from the boys ' 2007 self-titled album .
