This is the first single from English singer-songwriter Patrick Wolf 's fifth studio album .
Wolf said regarding the disc : `` The new album has a direct narrative about love and optimism surviving through adversity and recession .
I wanted to celebrate the love and hope I have found in the last few years . ''
Wolf explained : '' ` Time Of My Life ' is a song that I began writing at the end of a relationship in 2006 and then finished three years later during a temporary break-up in my current relationship . ''
Written and produced by Wolf , the song features strings recorded at Air Studios , London and was premiered on November 3 , 2010 on Nick Grimshaw 's BBC Radio 1 show .
