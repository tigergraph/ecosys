This song is about Billy Corgan of the Smashing Pumpkins .
Billy tried to sue the band after `` Shine '' was a hit - he claimed they copied his song `` Rhinosauris . ''
However , `` Shine '' was featured on a Ed Roland demo back in 1987 long before Billy wrote his song .
The case was thrown out .
