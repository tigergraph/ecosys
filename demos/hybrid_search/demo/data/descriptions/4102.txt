This song was originally titled `` Steel Wheels . ''
They ended up using that as the name of the album , as it conveyed moving forward .
The Stones launched their first tour in 8 years after releasing the album , and this was the second single , after `` Mixed Emotions . ''
This was one of bass player Bill Wyman 's last recordings as a member of The Stones .
He left the band in 1992 .
The title reflected the state of The Stones at the time .
Mick Jagger and Keith Richards had released solo albums and were feuding , but they put aside their differences to record Steel Wheels .
