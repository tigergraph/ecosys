The closing track of Canadian singer-songwriter Kathleen Edwards ' fourth album , Voyageur , features guest vocals from Norah Jones .
Edwards told UK newspaper The Sun , about the moments after the `` Do n't Know Why '' singer sung on this tune .
She recalled : `` She had sung her parts in New York and when they got sent back to Toronto , I put the song on and tears just came to my eyes .
I was so elated to hear her voice with mine .
And it felt like a victory to know someone like her would sing with me . ''
