In this nostalgic song , Ringo sings about his life growing up in Liverpool , touring with Rory Storm and The Hurricanes , and joining The Beatles .
He left Liverpool to go on these adventures , but he made the city proud .
Dave Stewart from The Eurythmics wrote this with Ringo and helped produce the track .
-LRB- Read our interview with Dave Stewart . -RRB-
Ringo named the song and the album `` Liverpool 08 '' to celebrate the city 's selection as the 2008 European Capital of Culture .
Every year , the European Union selects a city for the award .
