Brandi Carlile is good friends with Miranda Lambert and sang at her wedding when she married Blake Shelton .
The Folk-Pop singer-songwriter penned this cut about a girl breaking up with her man because he is stuck in a rut , and also provided background vocals on the track .
Carlile is best known to many for penning `` The Story , '' which was a Hot 100 hit for both her in 2007 and Grey 's Anatomy actress Sara Ramirez four years later .
