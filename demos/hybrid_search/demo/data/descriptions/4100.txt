This song is centered around Jack 's Mannequin frontman Andrew McMahon 's experiences of being a musician .
During the track-by-track interview video he related the song 's theme to the 2008 documentary Man On Wire , which chronicles Philippe Petit 's 1974 high-wire walk between the Twin Towers of New York 's World Trade Center .
McMahon first introduced this song at a live performance in 2009 .
