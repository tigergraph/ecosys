In this song , Ciara sings the praises of her hometown of Atlanta .
Ludacris , who raps on the song , is also from Atlanta .
Dre & Vidal , who are the production team of Andre Harris and Vidal Davis , produced this track .
Showing an affinity for 2-letter titles , they also worked on Chris Brown 's `` Yo -LRB- Excuse Me Miss -RRB- . ''
This was part of the `` Crunk n ' B '' sound coming out of the US .
Ciara , who was enjoying a high-profile relationship with rapper Bow Wow at the time , was marketed as `` The Princess of Crunk . ''
