This tale of a break-up in which no party is particularly loveable is the first single from Babybird , a.k.a. Stephen Jones ' sixth album , Ex-Maniac .
Depp also directed the video , which was filmed at Hatfield House in Hertfordshire , the home of the home of the 7th Marquess of Salisbury .
Hollywood star Johnny Depp plays guitar on this track and he managed to complete his parts in a few hours .
Depp told Q magazine March 2010 : `` Babybird is a national treasure , if you 're British .
For everyone else , he 's a diamond waiting to be found . ''
Ex-Maniac was produced by Bruce Witkin , who used to play in a band with Depp .
It was the Hollywood star who introduced Jones to the Grammy-nominated producer .
