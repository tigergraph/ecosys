A balladeer wrote this song for Matthew Shepard , who was a gay American student at the University of Wyoming who was murdered near Laramie on the night of October 6 , 1998 .
Shepard was kidnapped and left for dead until a bicyclist discovered him .
At the time of discovery , Shepard was still alive , but in a coma .
He died a few days later from severe head injuries .
