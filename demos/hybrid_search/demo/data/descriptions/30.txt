This is a single from Mariah Carey 's 13th studio album Angels Advocate aimed at Urban A/C radio formats .
The song is a new version of a track from Carey 's 2009 LP , Memoirs of an Imperfect Angel , with additional vocals from Carey 's fellow Island Def Jam artist Ne-Yo .
Angels Advocate is a collection of remixed duets with some of Carey 's favorite artists , performing some tracks from her 2009 album Memoirs of an Imperfect Angel plus several songs which did not make the original listing .
