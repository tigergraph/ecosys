This `` truth hurts '' ode to an unfaithful lover is the fifth and final single from British singer Paloma Faith 's debut album , Do You Want the Truth or Something Beautiful ? .
Faith relives one of her old occupations in the song 's music video , which sees her play the role of a bored magician 's assistant / lover .
Before she became a successful recording artist , Faith studied for a MA in theatre directing at Central Saint Martins College of Art and Design .
She funded her studies by working various part-time jobs , one of which was a magician 's assistant .
