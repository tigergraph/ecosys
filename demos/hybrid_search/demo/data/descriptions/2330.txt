The Fall is an album recorded and mixed on Gorillaz founder Damon Albarn 's Apple iPad during the band 's American leg of their Escape to Plastic Beach World Tour .
This lament recalling America 's vast great interior was recorded in Boston , Massachusetts on October 5 , 2010 .
Fictional Gorillaz lead vocalist 2D stated on episode 5 of the Pirate Radio broadcasts on the band 's website that he wrote this song after he saw a set of revolving doors in a hotel and it reminded him of how far he was from home and how out of place he was .
