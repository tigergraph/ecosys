This is the debut single by Jamaican singer Laza Morgan .
It is also the first single from the soundtrack to Step Up 3D , the third installment of the film franchise .
Laza Morgan is the son of Reggae legend Denroy Morgan and a former member of the popular Jamaican group LMS .
He penned this song with , J. Bunetta and S. ` Pages ' Mehner .
The singer describes it as , `` A breath of fresh air !
It 's a feel good love song , perfect for all seasons and a song anyone can sing . ''
