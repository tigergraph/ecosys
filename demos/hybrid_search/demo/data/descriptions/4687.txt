This smooth , down-tempo ballad is a single from American actor and singer Jamie Foxx 's fourth studio album , Best Night of My Life .
The Canadian rapper Drake , who also penned this track , spits a verse .
The song 's music video was directed by Chris Robinson and Drake ; model Jessica Burciaga features as Foxx 's love interest .
The clip captures the range of emotions and drama the singer experiences after falling in love with a volatile woman .
The song finds Foxx trying to convince himself that he has found the right girl as he always falls for that particular type .
