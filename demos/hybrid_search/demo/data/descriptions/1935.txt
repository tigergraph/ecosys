Growing up in Oakland , California , Raphael Saadiq was the youngest boy among 14 siblings .
The closing track of his fifth studio album Stone Rollin ' pays tribute to the personal and professional mentors who helped raise him .
Saadiq told the UK newspaper The Daily Telegraph : `` I wanted to send a message to people who deserved it -- uncles , baseball coaches , music teachers .
People who build guidelines for you to go the right way . ''
