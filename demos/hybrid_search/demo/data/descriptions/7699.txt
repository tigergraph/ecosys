Buckcherry 's sixth album , Confessions , is based on the seven deadly sins , and is also influenced by vocalist Josh Todd 's childhood and transformation into adulthood .
This angst-driven song finds Todd singing about anger .
`` They say it 's the quiet ones you got ta look out for , you know why ?
Because they keep all their wrath inside . ''
said the frontman about the track .
