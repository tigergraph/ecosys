This is the lead single from the Florida-based pop-rockers We the Kings third album .
The band premiered the song on April 8 , 2011 and was made available on iTunes four days later on April 12th .
The feel-good tune is all about living and loving in the moment .
Frontman Travis Clark told AOL Music : '' ` Friday Is Forever ' captures everything that our fans have loved about WTK - catchy melodies , upbeat music , and sing-a-long lyrics . ''
