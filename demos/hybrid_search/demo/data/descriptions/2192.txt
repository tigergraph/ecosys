This is the fourth single from the southern Hip-Hop artist Waka Flocka Flame 's debut album , Flockaveli .
It was released on February 15 , 2011 .
This was produced by Virginia beatsmith Lex Luger , who also manned the boards on Waka 's second single `` Hard in Da Paint . ''
The song features Brick Squad affiliate Kebo Gotti and finds the duo rapping about partying hard .
