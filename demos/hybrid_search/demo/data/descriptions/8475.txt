This upbeat Stargate production is the first single from Jamaican-born , American-based singer , Sean Kingston 's third studio album .
The dancehall-flavored song features American rapper Nicki Minaj , one of a number of high-profile collaborations she 's featured in .
They include `` Bedrock '' as part of Young Money and `` My Chick Bad '' with Ludacris .
The song samples Rihanna 's `` Te Amo , '' which was also produced by Stargate .
Kingston returned to his home island to film the song 's music video with Minaj .
The Little X directed clip was shot in Ocho Rios , Jamaica .
Several local Jamaican entertainers make cameo appearances , including Shaggy and Wayne Marshall .
