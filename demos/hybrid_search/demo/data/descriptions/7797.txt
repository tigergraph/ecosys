Mike Rosenberg was the founder , main vocalist and songwriter of the four-piece folk rock group Passenger that were based in Brighton , England .
After the breakup of the band , Rosenberg kept the moniker Passenger and took to busking for a solo music career .
He told VH1 in a 2013 interview this song reflects his itinerant lifestyle : `` I have n't had a house for five years , '' he said , `` I 've just been sleeping in hostels or hotels or mates ' couches or whatever .
You know , living like that kind of frees you up .
You just start looking at life differently . ''
