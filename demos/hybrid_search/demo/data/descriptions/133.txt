This is the third single by American rock band OneRepublic , written and produced by frontman Ryan Tedder for the group 's second studio album Waking Up .
Originally , the song `` Good Life '' was supposed to serve as the third single , but due to `` Marchin On '' being selected as German TV channel ZDF 's promotional FIFA 2010 World Cup song , `` Good Life '' had to be pushed back to being the fourth single .
Producer and rapper Timbaland included a remixed version of the song on his 2009 album , Shock Value 2 .
