This song originally featured on a 2012 EP of the same name but was re-recorded by Aplin for inclusion on her debut album , English Rain .
She told Digital Spy regarding the new version : `` It 's slightly bigger-sounding than before and it 's exactly how I wanted it to be in the first place . ''
The track was written by Aplin with the collaboration of the former frontman of Rooster , Nicholas Atkinson .
The Indie-Rockers had three UK Top 20 hits in the mid-2000s including `` Staring At The Sun , '' which peaked at # 5 .
The song was produced by Mike Spencer whose other credits include Kylie Minogue 's UK chart-topper `` Spinning Around '' and Alex Clare 's transatlantic hit `` Too Close . ''
