This is the second single by rapper Snoop Dogg , from his eleventh studio album Doggumentary .
The song was released in the iTunes Store on March 8 , 2011 .
The video was directed by Dylan Brown , who was also responsible for Snoop 's visuals for `` New Year 's Eve '' and `` Wet . ''
The song features rapper T-Pain and was produced by Scott Storch .
It features samples from Yazoo 's 1980 's hit `` Situation . ''
