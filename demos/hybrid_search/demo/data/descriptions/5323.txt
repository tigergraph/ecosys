Speaking with the French publication Le Nouvel Observateur , Daft Punk explained that this is a song about lost love that is being sung by an android .
`` We sing with vocoders , '' they said .
`` At a time when human voices are processed to become robotic , we found exciting to make a more humane as possible robotic voice .
The idea of artificial intelligence approaches the man ... An emotion of something that is not human but tries to be . ''
