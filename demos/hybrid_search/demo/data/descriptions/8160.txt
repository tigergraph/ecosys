Maybe the closest Matchbox 20 has come to recording a party anthem , this song was released as the band 's third single from their North album .
The song 's music video features LA-based Australian actress Sharni Vinson who is best known for her role as the female lead in the third instalment of the Step Up film series , titled Step Up 3D .
Also co-starring is her real-life boyfriend Kellan Lutz , who many will recognize from his role of Emmett Cullen in the Twilight film series .
Hinder fans will also know Lutz from his playing the troublesome boyfriend in the band 's `` Without You '' video .
