When asked by American Songwriter magazine what was a lyric that he is especially proud of , Howard replied the start of this song is one he is particularly pleased with .
He explained that it , `` always reminds me of home when I sing it so I always think it 's quite a good little lyric because it pictures a place for me instantly . ''
Howard added : `` It 's nothing profound , it 's just ` and the birds still sing outside these windows where we sat together . '
It always reminds me of my little window at home . ''
