The opening track of This Modern Glitch was also the first song written to make the final track listing .
Lead singer and guitarist Matthew Murphy told Spinner : `` It was originally much longer but got chopped down in pre-production with Rich Costey .
The song is about the close connection and similarities between hate and love . ''
The tune was released as the fifth single from the album on August 14 , 2011 .
The Mark Staubach directed video links with the song title by getting the band to play the song in a dilapidated hospital emergency room .
