This was released as the second official single of T.I. 's second EP , Paper Trail : Case Closed .
The song was originally set to appear on the extended version of Paper Trail , which was ultimately shelved by the record label .
On this Jim Jonsin and Danja Hills produced party jam , T.I. contrasts between the life he will have to give up in prison and his situation at the time he recorded this track .
The music video was shot in T.I. 's hometown of Atlanta , shortly before he started his year long prison sentence for gun charges .
It was directed by Erik White and shows the rapper spending his final hours before he goes behind bars .
