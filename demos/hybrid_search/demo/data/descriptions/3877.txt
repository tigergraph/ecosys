M.A.C.C. is made up of Pearl Jam 's Mike McCready and Jeff Ament and Soundgarden 's Chris Cornell and Matt Cameron .
The name of the band is the initials of all their last names .
It is the only release by M.A.C.C. and it appears on a tribute to Hendrix that also featured artists like Eric Clapton and the Spin Doctors .
This is a cover of a Jimi Hendrix song .
That song was part of an album Hendrix was working on at the time of his death .
The members of this band had collaborated on Temple of the Dog earlier in the decade .
