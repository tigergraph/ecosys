Robert `` Meek Mill '' Williams was raised in the North Philadelphia section of the city with his sister by his mother , after his father died from a gunshot when he was just 5 .
This Boi-1da-produced track finds Williams spitting rhymes about his desire to take revenge regarding the death of his dad .
`` You ripped my family apart and made my mama cry .
So when I see you ni -- a it 's gon na be a homicide , '' he tells the unknown murderer .
