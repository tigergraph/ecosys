This is Dagenham native Neo Soul singer McLean 's first major label single .
He first emerged in 2006 with `` Broken , '' which has since received over 5 million hits on Youtube and 20 million plays on MySpace .
McLean told 4Music 's Colin Gentry : `` It 's about that special someone who you want to spend your life with and give your name to .
It 's a big R&B song . ''
Originally produced by LA producers Slick and Magic in London , this song was completed in Los Angeles by Dylan ' 3D ' Dresdow , the producer behind Black Eyed Peas ' hits `` Boom Boom Pow '' and `` I Got ta Feelin ' . ''
McLean is brother to the footballer Aaron McLean , and cousin to '90s pop sensation Bitty McLean .
