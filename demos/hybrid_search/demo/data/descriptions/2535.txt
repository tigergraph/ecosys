This electropop jam finds Nicki nursing heartbreak as she laments a two-timing ex .
She wishes bad karma on her former lover as she sings , `` I hope you lay down in your sleep and you choke on every lie you told , and when you 're reaching out for me you 'll see you reap everything you sow . ''
Los Angeles-based Turkish-American record producer `` Oak '' Felder and his partner , Andrew `` Pop '' Wansel , provided the song 's beat .
The pair also helmed Minaj 's singles `` Your Love '' and `` Right By My Side . ''
