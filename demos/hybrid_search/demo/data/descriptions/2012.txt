This is a single from Los Angeles born R&B singer-songwriter Miguel 's debut album , All I Want Is You .
He penned the song as a pledge of his devotion to an ex-girlfriend after cheating on her .
`` I believed we could overcome adversity , obviously unbeknownst to her , '' Miguel told MTV News .
`` But I wrote a record for her about how perfect we were for each other . ''
Miguel told The Boombox why he wrote about his own romantic escapades on his debut album .
`` On All I Want Is You , I discussed my experience in my last three or four relationships .
It was a great way to introduce myself because I think love brings us all together , '' he said .
