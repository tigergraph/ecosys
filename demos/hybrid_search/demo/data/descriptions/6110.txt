Badu started writing her New Amerykah series in the mid 2000s and recorded this track , which was co-produced by her late friend J Dilla , on September 19 , 2007 .
She explained in a pre-release listening session that she remembers the date as it 's the birthday of her long-time boyfriend and the father of her third child , Mars Merkaba , Jay Electronica .
The song contains a portion of the early '70s funk number `` Take Me '' by Fabulous Souls .
