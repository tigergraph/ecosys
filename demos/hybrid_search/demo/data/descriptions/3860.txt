On this song Adam Young aka Owl City sings that he has , `` found a new hope from above . ''
He explained to Relevant Magazine that this is a spiritual reference .
Said Young : `` I cling dearly to the hope and joy that is found in Jesus Christ .
My faith is a big part of who I am and why I make music .
All I have belongs to Him . ''
Young explained to Female First : `` Tidal Wave is about running hard and racing for the prize with both eyes locked on the finish line so that one day , the words ` Well done , good and faithful servant ' might be heard . ''
This features guest vocals by Relient K singer Matt Thiessen .
