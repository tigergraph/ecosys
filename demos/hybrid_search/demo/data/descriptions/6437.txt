This song was originally released as Krule 's debut single on House Anxiety records in April 2010 , when the singer was called Zoo Kid .
It was recorded and mixed in his bedroom with his friends Francis North and Thelonious McCab .
The tune was later included on King Krule 's 2013 debut album , 6 Feet Beneath the Moon .
The song takes its title from a 1982 pencil-on-paper piece by Jean Michel Basquiat .
The late New York neo-expressionist artist is repeatedly referenced by American rappers .
Jay-Z , for instance , is a devotee of Basquiat and has name-checked him in his rhymes on several occasions .
