This features Timbaland 's protégé Keri Hilson along with Nicole Scherzinger of The Pussycat Dolls .
Timbaland and Hilson wrote `` Wait A Minute '' for The Pussycat Dolls , which peaked at # 28 in the US in 2007 .
Hilson also featured on 3 other tracks from the Shock Value album : `` The Way I Are , '' `` Hello '' and `` Miscommunication . ''
