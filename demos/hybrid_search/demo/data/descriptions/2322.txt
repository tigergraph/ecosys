This track features on the iTunes exclusive digital EP version of `` Every Teardrop is a Waterfall . ''
Coldplay 's web site Question and Answer section The Oracle states that the song was inspired by a documentary of the same title about two families being forced out of Burma into an entirely new world in the UK .
The documentary was directed by Mat Whitecross who helmed the `` Every Teardrop Is A Waterfall '' music video as well as a few previous ones by the band .
