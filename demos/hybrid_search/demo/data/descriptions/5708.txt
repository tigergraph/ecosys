The Good , The Bad , The Sexy is the ninth studio album by American R&B singer Joe .
The record earns its title by being a compilation of tracks fitting all of these descriptions .
Discussing the songs on the album that fit into the three categories with Billboard magazine , Joe said this track represents the good .
He said : `` It means we do n't have to have sex tonight , we could just cuddle and hold each other .
It sounds corny to a lot of people but I do n't mind doing that with my girl and that 's the good . ''
