This song was released as a free download by Radiohead from their official website , just days after it was leaked on the Internet .
The band posted a statement on their site saying that they have been working on the song for some time and would be performing it at their summer festival concerts .
The download came with various artwork pictures by vocalist Thom Yorke and Radiohead associate , the writer and artist Stanley Donwood .
It came with instructions that the artwork should be printed on tracing paper and put `` in an order that pleases you . ''
