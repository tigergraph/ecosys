The vintage black-and-white video finds will surrounded by a full band performing the song on a tiered podium , while filmed through a 1920s vintage filter .
He is accompanied by a flapper girl who recounts how she shot him down .
Critics have pointed out the similarities between a portion of this song and `` Epic '' by Quintino & Sandro Silva .
