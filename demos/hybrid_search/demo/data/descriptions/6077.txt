Lynyrd Skynyrd 's Gary Rossington and Johnny Van Zant penned this song with Blair Daly .
The Nashville-based songwriter has had hit singles on many different formats including Country , Top 40 and AC .
They include Rascal Flatts ' `` Stand '' and Uncle Kracker 's `` Smile . ''
Regarding the song 's meaning , Rossington told Classic Rock magazine : `` It is about a crazy girl , but it also has that double meaning of weed . ''
