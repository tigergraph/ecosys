Boo Radleys were named after the reclusive character in novel To Kill a Mockingbird who is feared by the neighborhood children as some kind of freak .
This track from The Boo Radleys Giant Steps album finds frontman Martin Carr recalling his adolescent ennui and frustration .
He told NME : `` You 've got no money and a crap job .
I 'd just sit in my room playing records . ''
