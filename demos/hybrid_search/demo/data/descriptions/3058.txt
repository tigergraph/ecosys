Frontman Antony Genn told The Sun April 17 , 2009 : `` I 'm not saying it 's the greatest song ever written , it 's not going to change the world , but if they put it on the radio , builders will hum it as they hang doors .
It 's got a catchy chorus and catchy is what we can do . ''
See the Light was the first release on artist Damien Hirst 's label Is Good Ltd. .
The Hours are Genn , who previously played in Pulp , Elastica and Joe Strummer 's Mescaleros , and former Black Grape keyboard player Martin `` Slatts '' Slattery .
