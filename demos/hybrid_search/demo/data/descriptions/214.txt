Guitarist Serge Pizzorno told Q magazine May 2009 : `` I wrote this when I was 20 .
It just felt like a good way to end the album .
It 's got a real Lou Reed , Perfect Day sound . ''
This uplifting track features a gospel choir and handclaps .
In an interview with the New Musical Express January 17 , 2009 , Pizzorno told how gospel singers were recruited in Los Angeles to get this song a `` sound like Elvis ' Sun recordings . ''
