This Red bonus track was penned by Taylor with her regular producer Nathan Chapman .
It features backing vocals from Taylor 's friend , Liz Huett , whom the Nashville star first met in a meet and greet line , when she was performing at a club .
Liz told her that she wanted to be a singer so Taylor brought her into her dressing room and gave her some advice .
After moving to Nashville , Liz successfully auditioned to be Taylor 's backup singer and has since toured with her all over the world with her .
