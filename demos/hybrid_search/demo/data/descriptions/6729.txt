George confirmed to the London Times October 3 , 2008 that he 's an Obama supporter , `` but the song 's personal : it 's about addiction , getting clean . ''
He added that the second verse is for Amy Winehouse personally .
`` Yes We Can '' was something Barack Obama said repeatedly in his speeches as he ran for president in 2008 .
His supporters rallied around the phrase , and will.i.am produced a popular video based on his speech .
The song features a plaintive plea , `` Please forgive these crimes against myself . ''
The London Times asked George who he is asking for forgiveness ?
The singer replied : `` Myself , of course -- although with my addiction it was only later I realized that I was hurting my family and friends . ''
