The title of the closing track of American rock quintet Mayday Parade 's third album is a quote by Jane Smith , played by Angelina Jolie , from the 2005 action movie Mr. & Mrs. Smith .
This is not the first time the band has taken a song name from that particular movie .
The track `` Champagne 's for Celebrating -LRB- I 'll Have a Martini -RRB- '' , from their debut album , was a quote by Brad Pitt 's character , John Smith .
