This bittersweet Christmas single by the Manchester based synthpop twosome Hurts was released on December 14 , 2010 .
The duo described the song to The X Factor website as being , `` about the worst Christmas of our lives , which we 're releasing on the best Christmas of our lives . ''
The video features the Hurts pair solemnly walking in the snow to a funeral and features appearances by some former X Factor contestants .
The duo described the clip to The X Factor website as `` like a Hurts Christmas party ... in a cemetery . ''
