This song is about the importance for Fabolous of being a presence in his son 's life .
He explained to Billboard magazine : `` It 's a personal record for me because in between the last album and this album I had a son , and so on this track I talk about my relationship with my son and with my father . ''
Loso 's Way is a concept album , inspired by the 1993 Al Pacino movie Carlito 's Way .
In the film , Carlito was expecting to become a father before the final denouement when his right-hand man betrays him .
One time member of the English R&B duo Floetry , Marsha Ambrosius , provides featured vocals on this song .
