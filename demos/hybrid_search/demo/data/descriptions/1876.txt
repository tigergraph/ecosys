This is the second single from Atlanta-based R&B and soul singer-songwriter Lyfe Jennings ' fourth album , I Still Believe .
The track was inspired by radio personality/comedian Steve Harvey 's book , Act Like a Lady , Think Like a Man : What Men Really Think About Love , Relationships , Intimacy , and Commitment , which advises women on how men really think and feel .
In the song Jennings outlines some facts and figures about men -LRB- `` 25 % of all men ca n't be faithful '' -RRB- and outlines some relationship rules for women -LRB- `` Be the person you wan na find '' -RRB- .
