This is a track from Black Gives Way to Blue , Alice in Chains ' first album in nearly fourteen years .
It is also the band 's first release first with singer-guitarist William DuVall , who replaced the late Layne Stayley .
DuVall had previously worked with Comes with the Fall and as part of Jerry Cantrell 's touring band for his solo work .
Straight away on this opening track the band addresses the loss of Stayley as they acknowledge their personal pain and talk about moving on .
