Pistol Annies is an all-female trio comprising Miranda Lambert and fellow songwriters Angaleena Presley and Ashley Monroe .
Lambert explained the inspiration for this song to Billboard magazine : `` I started thinking about how Southern boys just have a certain charm about them , '' she said .
`` It 's one of our favorite songs on the album .
Every time I play it live anywhere , especially below the Mason-Dixon , people love it . ''
