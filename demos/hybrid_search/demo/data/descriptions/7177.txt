This haunting electro ballad is the fourth US single by Kelly Rowland from her self-titled album .
The song was co - produced by David Guetta , who previously collaborated with Rowland on the singles , `` When Love Takes Over '' and `` Commander . ''
Another producer called Jonas also has a production credit .
Sam Watters and Andre Merrit wrote the track .
The single was premiered on BBC Radio 1 on August 18 , 2010 where it was announced as Rowland 's second UK single from the album following on from `` Commander . ''
