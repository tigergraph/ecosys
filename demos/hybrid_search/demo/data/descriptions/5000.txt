This Progressive Rock track features Taylor Brandt on violin , Zachery Veilleux on piano and Grant McFarland on cello .
Guitarist and principal songwriter JB Brubaker told The Alternative Press : `` It features a classical interlude that happens to be one of my favorite moments on the album .
I really like this song because of its complete lack of structure and how it just rambles on . ''
Brubaker said regarding the song 's meaning : `` Lyrically , this song talks about keeping an open mind and not judging other people who are different from you . ''
