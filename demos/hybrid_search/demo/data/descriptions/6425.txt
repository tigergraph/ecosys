This song is a vivid description of a car accident that lands the singer in the hospital , where he counts his blessings .
When he spoke with Adam Young of Owl City , he told us that it was based on a true story .
Said Young : `` I had a friend who was in an accident and I watched his recovery change his outlook on life .
I feel like we could all use a disaster here and there to wake us up . ''
