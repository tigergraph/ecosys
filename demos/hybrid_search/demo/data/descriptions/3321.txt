In this song , Notorious B.I.G. raps about his humble beginnings and unlikely rise to fame .
He grew up in Brooklyn at a time when rap was just emerging in that area and widely considered a passing fad .
In the song , he mentions several Hip-Hop pioneers who influenced him , including Marley Marl and Mr. Magic .
