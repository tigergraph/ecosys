Justin debuted this acoustic tune during his Toronto-based TV concert special , Justin Bieber : Home for the Holidays , which aired on December 22 , 2011 on MuchMusic and CTV .
Justin explained to the crowd , before unveiling the song : `` Basically , the concept is : Even though when times get hard with your girlfriend or boyfriend , when times get hard , you got ta just let them know that in the end , everything 's gon na be alright . ''
