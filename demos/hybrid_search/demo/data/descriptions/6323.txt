The lead single from 50 Cent 's fifth studio album , Street King Immortal features Alicia Keys on its triumphant chorus .
50 's longtime collaborator , Dr. Dre joins in as well , as the pair rap about their accomplishments .
The song was premiered with DJ Camilo on New York City radio station Hot 97 and released on July 27 , 2012 .
Alicia Keys also laid down a solo version , in similar fashion to her recording of `` Empire State of Mind -LRB- Part II -RRB- , '' which followed her collaboration with Jay-Z .
Keys ' rendition was released by her husband Swizz Beatz on the internet on June 28 , 2012 .
Dr. Dre also produced the track , which was mixed by another rap superstar , Eminem .
