This is a track on Scottish DJ/producer Calvin Harris ' second album , Ready For The Weekend .
This song features guest vocalist Ayah Marar .
The Jordan-born singer first collaborated with Harris on his 2004 song `` Let Me Know , '' which appears on The Unabombers ' Electric Soul 2 album .
Her voice can also be heard on the chorus of Jack Penate 's 2009 hit single `` Tonight 's Today . ''
