This is the second single from American singer-songwriter and producer Kevin Rudolf 's second studio album , To The Sky .
The song features rapper Flo Rida and was released on August 17 , 2010 .
Rudolf told the story of the song to Artist Direct : `` Honestly , ' I Made It ' came out , and I was like , ` I 've got an album together because this record is going up the charts . '
I had some ideas , and ` You Make the Rain Fall ' was one of them .
It just had the hook .
I built the song around that . ''
