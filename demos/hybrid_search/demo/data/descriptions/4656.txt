This is the opening track from Barbadian singer Rihanna 's fourth studio album , Rated R .
The song is one of three on the album that was produced by British production duo , Chase & Status , the other two being `` Wait Your Turn '' and `` G4L . ''
Rihanna told The Daily Telegraph November 18 , 2009 : `` From when I first heard Chase & Status , I had to work with them .
These two guys are magic .
They make the grungiest , most amazing beats ever . ''
