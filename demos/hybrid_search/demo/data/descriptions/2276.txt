This Chuck Harmony produced motivational song is the second single from actress and R&B / soul singer Fantasia 's third studio album , Back To Me .
Rap and R&B music director Benny Boom helmed the song 's video , which was shot on October 8 and October 9 , 2010 in New York City .
It features Fantasia falling for the wrong guy but succeeding in keeping her emotions in check .
