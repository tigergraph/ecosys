This is the first single from Swedish DJ and Euro dance musician Basshunter 's follow-up disc to his chart-topping Now You 're Gone - The Album .
The song was given a worldwide premier live on Scott Mills 's BBC Radio 1 program on May 14 , 2010 .
The track was co-written by Danish songwriter and producers Cutfather , Thomas Troelsen and Engelina .
Cutfather is one half of the Danish production team Soulshock & Cutfather , who have produced or remixed music for many leading artists including JLS , Brandy , Santana , Christina Aguilera and Pixie Lott .
