This is the title track from Country-Pop group Rascal Flatts ' seventh studio album .
The song , which finds a way to bring freshness to the subject of love , was written by Neil Thrasher and Tom Shapiro , who have penned several other tunes for Rascal Flatts including the lead single of Nothing Like This , `` Why Wait . ''
The other writer was Michael Dulaney , who also co-wrote `` Close '' and `` Things That Matter '' on the trio 's Unstoppable album .
