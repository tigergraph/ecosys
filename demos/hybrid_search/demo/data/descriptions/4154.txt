This whistle-heavy track is the third single from the reissue of Euphoria , Enrique Iglesias 's ninth studio album .
The song premiered on NRJ radio September 9 , 2011 and was officially released on September 27 , 2011 .
Iglesias previously collaborated with Pitbull and RedOne on his 2010 hit single `` I Like It . ''
The song was produced and co-written by frequent Lady Gaga collaborator RedOne and features Cuban American rapper Pitbull .
It finds the two Latin recording artists celebrating being upbeat and enjoying life .
