End Times tells the story of a middle-aged divorced man , who equates his personal loss with the loss of integrity in the world he 's living in .
In this sparse and sad ballad , Eels leader Mark Oliver Everett compares love lost to a tiny bird flitting around a porch .
This is not the first time that Everett has penned a song about our feathered friends .
His 2000 track , I Like Birds is a tribute to his late mother who was an avid birdwatcher .
