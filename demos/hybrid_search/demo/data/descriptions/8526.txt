This is the title track of the eighth album from Scottish Rock band Texas .
The Conversation was the group 's first studio album of new material since Red Book in 2005 .
The accompanying music video stars singer Sharleen Spiteri and actor Peter Mullan , who is best known for his roles in Trainspotting and the Harry Potter film series .
Spiteri explained to The Guardian : `` Basically , I 'm supposed to be an art thief and Peter Mullan is supposed to be the boss of me , but someone 's got wind of our game and tries to intercept that .
And I 'm thinking he will get caught . ''
