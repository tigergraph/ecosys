This is the title track from Canadian Electropop group , Dragonette 's , third album .
The song was released on iTunes on April 3 , 2012 .
The song 's music video sees singer Martina Sorbara dancing around a laboratory and being studied by scientists .
Describing the clip , the band said : `` The creeps in this video will go to great extremes to ensure you 're always happy , even if it means leeching happiness from other unsuspecting humans and condensing it into pills to feed to you .
These weirdos think you need help keeping your thoughts light and your mind vacant .
We think you should Let It Go . ''
