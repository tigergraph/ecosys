This is the third single off Will Young 's fifth studio album , Echoes .
The song was co-written by Young along with Belgium-born French-based musician Pascal Gabriel , whose first commercial success was co-writing and producing S'Express ' 1988 # 1 hit `` Theme From S'Express . ''
The single was released on March 18 , 2012 .
The video sees Young trailed by lookalikes , who mimic his every move and are meant represent his inner demons following him .
The singer joked that he picked up the routine 's complex choreography in `` about an hour '' , before adding : `` And then it went on for another three days ! ''
