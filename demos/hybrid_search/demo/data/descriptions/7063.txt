This was inspired by early '50s rock .
Robert Plant put the lyrics together from parts of Elvis Presley songs .
Robert Plant considers this one of his favorites .
Plant sang this from a wheelchair because he was recovering from a car accident .
The band wrote this in about an hour at a studio in Germany .
They were under pressure to finish Presence so the Rolling Stones could use the studio .
This was released as a single in America , but it did not chart .
