This is the third single by American rapper Kreayshawn , from her debut album , Somethin' Bout Kreay .
The song 's message is `` stay true to yourself and go hard at chasing your dreams . ''
The Oakland rapper told MTV 's RapFix , `` With this track , I wanted to inspire people to just do their own thing . ''
`` Go Hard '' was released on July 24 , 2012 .
The comedic dream-like video was the brainchild of Kreayshawn and Los Angeles design collective Syndrome .
`` My favorite part about the video is that I always notice something different every single time I watch it .
It 's like going on a visual adventure , '' she told Rapfix .
