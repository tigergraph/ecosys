This was released by the Kaiser Chiefs as a one-off single as a stopgap before they started writing material for the follow-up to The Future is Medieval .
Chief songwriter and drummer Nick Hodgson told The Daily Star that he came up with lyrics , which take aim against the boorishness of modern society while walking his dog Betty .
`` I 'm on the run from the world really , '' he explained .
`` There are too many opinions out there , like on Twitter .
It 's full of rude , cruel people . ''
This was included on Start The Revolution Without Me , which was released for the American market .
In addition to this song , it contained several tracks from the physical release of The Future Is Medieval , and a few from the online version of the album .
