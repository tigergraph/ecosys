This is the opening track to The Appeal : Georgia 's Most Wanted , the third studio album by American rapper Gucci Mane .
The song features Bun B and was produced by Rodney `` Darkchild '' Jerkins , who 's also worked with the likes of Brandy , Destiny 's Child and Michael Jackson .
Gucci said of the song to MTV News : `` That 's the intro for my album .
It 's raw , it 's ` hood , but it 's different .
Of course me and Bun B , it 's underground , but it still has a lot of pizzazz to it . ''
